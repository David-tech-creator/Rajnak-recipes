---
title: Garlic Shrimp
date: '2026-05-23'
category: Quick & Easy
image: /images/recipes/garlic-shrimp.jpg
prepTime: '10 mins'
cookTime: '8 mins'
servings: 4
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 600 g large raw shrimp, peeled and deveined
  - 6 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  - 1 small red chili, finely sliced
  - 80 ml extra virgin olive oil
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - 2 tbsp dry sherry or white wine
  - 1 tbsp lemon juice
  - 2 tbsp flat-leaf parsley, chopped
  - 1 tsp flaky sea salt
  - Crusty bread, to serve
instructions:
  - Pat the shrimp dry and season with the salt.
  - Heat the olive oil in a wide pan over medium heat. Add the garlic and chili and cook gently for one minute — fragrant but not coloured.
  - Stir in the smoked paprika, then immediately add the shrimp in a single layer.
  - Cook 1.5 minutes, flip, and cook another 1.5 minutes until pink and just opaque.
  - Pour in the sherry and lemon juice. Bubble for 30 seconds.
  - Off the heat, scatter the parsley over the top and serve immediately, straight from the pan, with bread to mop up the garlicky oil.
---

The classic Spanish gambas al ajillo — eight minutes from cold pan to table. The hot oil is half the point; serve it sizzling.
