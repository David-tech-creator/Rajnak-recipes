prepTime: '2 mins'
cookTime: '3 mins'
servings: 1
kitchenNote: Never let the milk boil.
signoff: Tuck in.
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 1 tsp sugar
  - 2 tsp good cocoa powder
  - 1 tbsp hot water
  - 3 dl whole milk
instructions:
  - Stir the sugar and cocoa powder together in your mug until evenly mixed.
  - Add the hot water and stir to a smooth, glossy paste with no lumps.
  - Heat the milk separately in the microwave or a small saucepan until it foams on top but doesn't boil.
  - Pour a splash of hot milk into the cocoa paste and stir well. Then pour the loosened paste back into the rest of the milk and stir.
  - Drink immediately, while the foam is still on top.
---

Pappa's perfected method for the creamiest, frothiest hot chocolate. The trick is making the paste first so the cocoa actually dissolves — and never letting the milk boil.
