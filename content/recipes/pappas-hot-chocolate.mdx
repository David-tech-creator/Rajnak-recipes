Pappa's perfected method for the creamiest, frothiest hot chocolate.

## Instructions

1. Mix sugar and chocolate powder in a cup.
2. Add a little warm water until it becomes even.
3. Boil 3dl milk separately in microwave until it gets foam on top.
4. Add milk a little at first, pour and mix.
5. Then pour the mixture back into the milk.
