Grilled chicken served with fresh avocado and tomatoes, finished with a drizzle of balsamic vinegar. A simple and colorful dish.

## Instructions

1. Grill the chicken until cooked through.
2. Slice the avocado and tomatoes.
3. Arrange on a plate and drizzle with balsamic vinegar.
