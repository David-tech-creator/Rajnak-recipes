ingredients:
  - 400 g double crème de la Gruyère
  - 300 g mixed fresh berries (strawberries, raspberries, blueberries)
  - 8 small meringues, lightly crushed
  - 2 tbsp icing sugar
  - 1 tsp lemon juice
  - Fresh mint leaves, to garnish
instructions:
  - Rinse and dry the berries. Halve any larger strawberries.
  - Toss the berries with the icing sugar and lemon juice. Let macerate ten minutes so the juices come out.
  - Spoon a generous dollop of double crème into the bottom of each glass or bowl.
  - Top with the macerated berries and their juice.
  - Scatter the crushed meringues over the top and garnish with mint.
  - Serve immediately, before the meringues lose their crunch.
---

A Swiss treat from the Fribourg region: thick, almost-set double cream poured over berries. The crushed meringue is the family addition for crunch.
