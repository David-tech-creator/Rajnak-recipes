0.5cc salt

a little ground pepper

butter for roasting

1 small onion , finely chopped

200g button mushrooms , thinly sliced

1 cc lemon juice

1 dl white wine

2dl of cream

1 dl beef broth

1 tbsp cornstarch

salt, pepper , to taste

1 bunch flat-leaf parsley , finely chopped

How it’s made:

Preheat the oven to 60°C, slide in a dish and plates. Heat the butter well in

a frying pan. Add the meat and kidneys in portions, sprinkle with flour,

brown for approx. 3 mins, remove, salt, pepper, keep warm. Lower the heat,

pat the bottom of the pan dry.

Heat the butter well in the same frying pan. Brown the onion, mix the

mushrooms and lemon juice, add, simmer for approx. 2 minutes.

Moisten with the wine, reduce over medium heat. Mix the cream, broth and

cornflour well, add, bring to the boil. Lower the heat, leave to bubble gently

for approx. 3 mins until the sauce is velvety, adjust the seasoning. Add the

meat, kidneys and half the parsley, until well heated. Arrange the minced

meat on a dish, sprinkle with the remaining parsley.
