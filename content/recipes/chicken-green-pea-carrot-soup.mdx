A hearty family soup with tender chicken, vegetables, and handmade mannagrynssnockerli (semolina dumplings).

## Soup

Rapeseed oil in saucepan, heat it. Pour in the vegetable mix, add salt, fry it a little. Pour the chicken pieces shortly after, just fry it a little. Season with meat, vegetable or chicken broths - add 1.5 liter of water and 3 broth cubes. Cook until soft and cooked. Remove the foam that comes up on the surface - it will become more crystal clear.

## Mannagrynssnockerli

Take 1 egg, mix with a little salt. Pour in grits and mix until it becomes a soft dough. Divide the dough using a small teaspoon. Put it in the soup 5 minutes before the soup is done. Boil it for 5 min.
