  - 200-250g cottage cheese
  - 200-250g Feta cheese
  - Half an onion, grated
  - 100g creme fraiche (half a can)
  - 1 tablespoon soft butter
  - Salt
  - Red pepper powder
prepTime: 10 mins
cookTime: 0 mins
servings: 4

A traditional Hungarian cheese spread made with cottage cheese, feta, and a touch of red pepper. Simple to make and perfect served with fresh bread.

## Instructions

1. Mix all ingredients together until smooth.
2. Serve with bread.
