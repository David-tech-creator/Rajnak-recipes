---
title: Bruschetta Di Pomodori Tomato on Bread
date: '2026-05-23'
category: Quick & Easy
image: /images/recipes/bruschetta-pomodori.jpg
ingredients:
  - 8-10 large ripe red tomatoes with a lot of flavor
  - 18 fresh basil leaves
  - 1½ dl olive oil
  - salt and pepper
  - 1 clove of garlic
  - 6 large slices of Italian country bread
prepTime: ''
cookTime: ''
servings: 4
featured: false
---

1. Peel the tomatoes and chop the tomato flesh coarsely.

2. Shred half of the basil leaves and mix chopped tomatoes and shredded

basil in a bowl with the olive oil.

3. Stir and season with salt and pepper.

4. Toast the bread golden brown in the oven and rub in one side of the bread

with a halved clove of garlic.

5. Lightly salt. Spread the chopped tomato evenly on the bread slices and

garnish with the remaining basil leaves.

Tip: The basil can be replaced by parsley or shredded rocket salad.
