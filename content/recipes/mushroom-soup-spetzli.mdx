  - 1 onion cut up
  - 400-500 g champignons sliced and washed
  - 2l water
  - salt
  - 4 carrots
  - 1 tablespoon flour
  - 1 tablespoon Rapeseed oil
  - Paprika powder
  - 2-3 vegetable bouillon cubes
  - Fry onion in oil until colored
  - Add paprika powder
  - Add mushrooms
  - Add carrot slices
  - Stir
  - Add flour
  - Fill up with water
  - Add bouillon cubes
