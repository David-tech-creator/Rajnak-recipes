title: Croissants

prepTime: '30 mins'
cookTime: '15 mins'
servings: 8
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 1 roll ready-made croissant dough (8 triangles)
  - 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)
  - 2 tbsp butter, melted (optional, for brushing)
  - Flour, for dusting
instructions:
  - Heat the oven to 200°C and line a baking tray with parchment.
  - Unroll the croissant dough and separate it along the perforations into eight triangles.
  - Starting at the wide end of each triangle, roll up tightly toward the tip and curve gently into a crescent shape.
  - Arrange on the baking tray with the tip tucked underneath, leaving space between each. Brush with beaten egg.
  - Bake for 12–15 minutes, until deeply golden and puffed.
  - Brush with melted butter while still warm and serve straight away.
---

The cheat's croissant — a Sunday morning shortcut for when the bakery is closed. Eat warm with butter and jam.
