---
title: Cheesecake
date: '2023-11-15'
category: Family Recipes
image: /images/recipes/cheesecake.svg
ingredients:
  - 200g digestive biscuits
  - 100g butter, melted
  - 600g cream cheese
  - 150g sugar
  - 3 eggs
  - 1 tsp vanilla extract
  - 200ml sour cream
instructions:
  - Crush biscuits and mix with melted butter. Press into the base of a springform tin.
  - Beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth.
  - Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  - Stir in vanilla and sour cream.
  - Pour over the biscuit base and smooth the top.
  - Bake at 160°C for 45-50 minutes until set but still slightly wobbly in the center.
  - Cool in the oven with the door ajar, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
prepTime: 30 mins
cookTime: 50 mins
servings: 8
featured: true
---

This classic cheesecake has a smooth, creamy texture and a buttery biscuit base. It's perfect for special occasions or whenever you need a luxurious dessert.

## Tips for Success

- Make sure all ingredients are at room temperature before starting
- Don't overmix the batter after adding the eggs
- A water bath can help prevent cracks in the surface
- Allow plenty of time for cooling and chilling
