A twist on classic carbonara with added zucchini and tomatoes for extra freshness and color.

## Instructions

Cook pasta. Fry mini bacon pieces until crispy. Add sliced zucchini and halved mini tomatoes. Dissolve beef bouillon cube in a splash of water and add to the pan. Pour in cream, stir, and let simmer. Toss with cooked pasta and top with grated parmesan.
