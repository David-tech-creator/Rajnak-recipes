ingredients:
  - 4 slices good white bread (or brioche)
  - 50 g butter, plus more for spreading
  - 100 g löjrom (Kalix vendace roe)
  - 2 tbsp sour cream or crème fraîche
  - 0.5 small red onion, very finely chopped
  - 1 tbsp finely snipped chives
  - 1 lemon, cut in wedges
  - Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
instructions:
  - Cut the bread into rounds or neat squares, trimming the crusts.
  - Melt the butter in a frying pan over medium heat. Fry the bread on both sides until golden and crisp. Drain briefly on kitchen paper.
  - Place the warm toasts on plates and butter lightly.
  - Spoon a generous mound of löjrom onto each toast.
  - Top with a small spoon of sour cream, a sprinkle of red onion, and a scatter of chives.
  - Finish with a crack of black pepper and serve with lemon wedges to squeeze at the table.
---

The Swedish New Year's Eve toast — golden butter-fried bread, glossy orange Kalix roe, sour cream, sharp little dice of red onion. Drink it with very cold aquavit or champagne.
