ingredients:
  - 100 g Swedish Butter
  - 2½ dl Powdered sugar
  - 2 Egg
  - 1 dl Flour
  - 3 tablespoons Cocoa
  - 1 tsp Vanilla sugar
  - "Garnish: icing sugar"
  - "Side: whipped cream & strawberries"
prepTime: ''
cookTime: ''
servings: 4
featured: false
---

1. Set the oven to 175 °.

2. Melt the butter in a pot. Lift the saucepan off the plate.

3. Stir in sugar and eggs, mix well. Stir in the other ingredients so that

everything is well mixed.

4. Pour the batter into a greased and breaded form with a removable edge,

about 24 cm in diameter.

5. Bake in the middle of the oven for about 15 minutes. The cake will be low

with a fairly hard surface and a little sticky in the middle.

6. Let the cake cool. Powder over icing sugar. Serve with cream or ice cream

and fruit.
