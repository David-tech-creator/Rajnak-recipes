prepTime: '10 mins'

servings: 4
kitchenNote: Under the grill. Watch like a hawk.
signoff: Smaklig måltid.
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 8 slices country bread or pain de mie
  - 2 dl crème fraîche
  - 200 g Hungarian smoked sausage (kolbász), sliced
  - 200 g grated melting cheese (Gruyère, Emmentaler, or matured Gouda)
  - 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  - 2 tomatoes, thinly sliced
  - 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  - 1 tsp sweet Hungarian paprika
  - Black pepper, to taste
instructions:
  - Heat the oven to 220 °C and line a baking sheet with parchment.
  - Spread each slice of bread generously with crème fraîche.
  - Arrange slices of Hungarian sausage on top, then a few slices of pepper, tomato, and red onion.
  - Cover thickly with grated cheese, dust with paprika and a grind of pepper.
  - Bake on the top shelf for 8 to 12 minutes, until the cheese is bubbling and the bread is golden at the edges. Eat while hot.
---

The Swedish-Hungarian fusion snack the family has been making for as long as anyone remembers — what to put on the table when guests arrive and there's nothing planned. Use a proper smoked Hungarian sausage if you can find one.
