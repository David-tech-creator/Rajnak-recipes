A Swedish-Hungarian fusion snack of warm open-faced sandwiches loaded with cheese, Hungarian sausage, creme fraiche, and fresh vegetables. Quick and satisfying.

## Instructions

1. Top slices of bread with cheese, Hungarian sausage, creme fraiche, peppers, and tomatoes.
2. Bake in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
3. Serve warm.
