A comforting and creamy potato and leek soup finished with chives.

## Instructions

1. Shred leeks into 3-5mm thick slices. Divide potato into smaller pieces.
2. Melt butter in saucepan and sauté leeks.
3. Add potato and broth to saucepan.
4. Let cook 25-30 minutes.
5. Run soup in food processor or with stick mixer until creamy.
6. Pour in cream.
7. Cook on low heat, season with salt and pepper.
8. When serving, sprinkle with chives.
