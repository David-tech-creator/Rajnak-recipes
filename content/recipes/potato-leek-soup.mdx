---
title: 'Potato & Leek Soup'
date: '2022-02-17'
category: Found Recipes
image: /images/recipes/potato-leek-soup.jpg
prepTime: '15 mins'
cookTime: '30 mins'
servings: 4
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 500 g floury potatoes, peeled and cubed
  - 2 large leeks, white and pale green parts only
  - 3 tbsp butter
  - 6 dl water
  - 1 vegetable stock cube
  - 2 dl double cream
  - Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  - 5 tbsp finely chopped chives, to serve
instructions:
  - Halve the leeks lengthways, rinse well between the layers, and slice into 3–5 mm half-moons.
  - Melt the butter in a heavy saucepan over medium heat. Add the leeks, season with a pinch of salt, and sweat gently for 8 minutes, until soft but not coloured.
  - Add the potatoes, water, and crumbled stock cube. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer and cook for 25 minutes, until the potatoes fall apart at a press.
  - Blend smooth with a stick blender (or in batches in a jug blender).
  - Stir in the cream and warm gently over low heat. Taste and season with salt and plenty of black pepper.
  - Ladle into bowls and shower with chopped chives.
---

A silky, comforting soup that's mostly pantry. The chives at the end aren't optional — they give the bowl its lift.
