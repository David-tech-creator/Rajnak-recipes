Crispy breaded goat cheese served warm on a bed of fresh salad with honey drizzle.

## Instructions

1. Slice goat cheese into rounds and bread them.
2. Bake in oven at 220 degrees until crispy and golden.
3. Arrange iceberg salad, carrots, corn, and tomatoes on plates.
4. Place warm goat cheese on top.
5. Drizzle with honey.
