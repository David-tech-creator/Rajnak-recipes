Broccoli baked with bacon, white sauce, and melted cheese — a hearty gratin the whole family loves.

## Instructions

1. Boil broccoli in lightly salted water for about 1 minute. Drain.
2. Lay broccoli in a greased form with bacon slices.
3. **White sauce:** Melt butter, pour in milk thoroughly mixed with wheat flour. Cook 4-5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Pour white sauce over broccoli and bacon.
5. Sprinkle with grated cheese.
6. Bake at 225 degrees for 20-30 minutes.
