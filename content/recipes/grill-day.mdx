Grilled marinated pork — a family favorite for sunny days. The marinade of mustard and rapeseed oil keeps the meat juicy on the grill.

## Instructions

1. Cut pork into 1cm thick pieces.
2. Pound into 1/2cm thick pieces.
3. Mix marinade: mustard, rapeseed oil, salt, and pepper.
4. Brush marinade on pork.
5. Leave for a few hours (half day or a day).
6. Grill.
