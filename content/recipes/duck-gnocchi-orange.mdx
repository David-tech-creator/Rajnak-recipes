An elegant dish of seared duck breast served with gnocchi, rich brown sauce, and fresh orange slices for a beautiful contrast of flavors.

## Instructions

1. Sear the duck breast and cook to your preferred doneness.
2. Prepare the gnocchi according to package directions.
3. Make or heat the brown sauce.
4. Slice the oranges.
5. Plate the duck with gnocchi, drizzle with brown sauce, and garnish with orange slices.
