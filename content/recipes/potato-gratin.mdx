A rich and creamy potato gratin with garlic and melted cheese — a perfect side dish.

## Instructions

1. Peel and slice raw potato thin.
2. Rub gratin dish with pressed garlic.
3. Layer potato slices in the dish. Add salt and black pepper.
4. Pour cream so potatoes are almost covered.
5. Bake at 175 degrees for 30 minutes.
6. Remove, sprinkle with grated cheese.
7. Bake another 25 minutes at 200 degrees.
8. Cover with foil towards end if getting too dark.
