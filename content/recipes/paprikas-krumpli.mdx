---
title: Sausage and Potato Stew (Paprikás Krumpli)
date: '2022-03-14'
category: Family Recipes
image: /images/recipes/paprikas-krumpli.jpg
prepTime: '15 mins'
cookTime: '40 mins'
servings: 4
kitchenNote: Add paprika off the heat, never on it.
signoff: Jó étvágyat.
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 2 tbsp lard or neutral oil
  - 2 large yellow onions, finely chopped
  - 2 tbsp sweet Hungarian paprika
  - 1 green pepper, sliced
  - 1 ripe tomato, diced
  - 1 kg waxy potatoes, peeled and cut into 3-cm chunks
  - 400 g Hungarian sausage (kolbász) or smoked chorizo, cut into 1-cm coins
  - 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
  - 1 tsp salt, plus more to taste
  - Black pepper, to taste
  - 700 ml hot water or light stock
  - Sour cream, to serve
instructions:
  - Melt the lard in a heavy pot over medium heat. Add the onions and cook gently for 10 minutes until soft and golden.
  - Pull the pot off the heat and stir in the paprika — it must not burn or it turns bitter. Add the garlic, green pepper, and tomato and stir back over low heat for 2 minutes.
  - Pour in the hot water, season with salt and pepper, and bring to a simmer.
  - Add the potatoes and simmer, covered, for 20 minutes.
  - Add the sliced sausage and continue to simmer, uncovered, for another 10–15 minutes, until the potatoes are tender and the sauce has thickened into a rich, red gravy.
  - Taste, adjust the salt, and serve in deep bowls with a spoonful of sour cream and pickled cucumbers on the side.
---

A classic Hungarian one-pot stew of sausage and potatoes in a paprika-rich sauce. The kind of dish that's better the next day and asks for nothing more than crusty bread.
