ingredients:
  - 5 tablespoons cocoa
  - 2½ teaspoons vanilla sugar
  - 100g butter
  - 1dl flour
  - ½ teaspoon salt
prepTime: ''
cookTime: ''
servings: 4
featured: false
---

1. Set the oven to 175 degrees

2. Melt the butter

3. Whisk eggs and sugar until porous

4. Mix (gently) with flour, cocoa, vanilla sugar, salt and butter

5. Pour the batter into a breaded form

6. Bake for 15-20 minutes

7. Allow the draft cake to cool before serving with sprinkles and strawber-

ries / raspberries
