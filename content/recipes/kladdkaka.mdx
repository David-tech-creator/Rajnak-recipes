ingredients:
  - 100 g butter
  - 2 eggs
  - 3 dl caster sugar
  - 1 dl plain flour
  - 5 tbsp cocoa powder
  - 2 tsp vanilla sugar
  - 0.5 tsp salt
  - Icing sugar, to dust
  - Whipped cream and strawberries or raspberries, to serve
instructions:
  - Heat the oven to 175 °C. Grease a 22-cm round tin with butter and dust with breadcrumbs or cocoa.
  - Melt the butter in a small saucepan and set aside to cool slightly.
  - Whisk the eggs and sugar in a bowl until pale and porous, about two minutes.
  - Fold in the flour, cocoa, vanilla sugar, and salt, then stir in the melted butter until just smooth — do not overmix.
  - Pour the batter into the tin and bake in the middle of the oven for 15–18 minutes. The surface should be set but the centre still soft and gooey.
  - Let the cake cool in the tin so it stays sticky in the middle. Dust with icing sugar and serve with whipped cream and berries.
---

The Swedish sticky cake — fudgy in the middle, faintly crisp on top. Underbake rather than overbake: the gooey centre is the whole point. A summer dessert with cold cream and warm strawberries.
