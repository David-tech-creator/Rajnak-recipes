---
title: Pavlova Cake with Kiwi
date: '2026-05-23'
category: Found Recipes
image: /images/recipes/pavlova-kiwi.jpg
ingredients:
  - 6 egg whites
  - 3dl sugar
  - 1 1/2 teaspoon white wine vinegar
  - 1 tablespoon potato starch
  - 1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla sugar
  - Kiwi
  - Oven at 275 degrees
  - Whisk egg white hard
  - Sugar added little by little, whisked hard
  - Vinegard whisked into it too
  - Stir in carefully potato starch and vanilla sugar
  - Spread the dough on a platter
  - Ovenproof paper 22cm in diameter round shape
  - Add in oven at bottom
  - Turn down oven heat to 100 degrees, let it sit inside 1 1/2 hours
  - 3dl whipped cream whisked
  - added to cake once cake is cool
  - cover / decorate with kiwi slices
prepTime: ''
cookTime: ''
servings: 4
