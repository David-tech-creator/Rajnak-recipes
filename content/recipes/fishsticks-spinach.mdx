A comforting family meal of crispy fishsticks with creamy spinach in a homemade white sauce.

## Instructions

1. Cook the fishsticks according to package directions.
2. Make the white sauce: heat a tablespoon of butter in a saucepan. When melted and getting color, pour in a milk-flour mixture (mix milk and flour together in a glass bowl first).
3. Mix constantly until the sauce has thickened and bubbled.
4. Remove from the stove.
5. Mix in the spinach, put back on the stove, and stir until heated through.
6. Serve the spinach alongside the fishsticks.
