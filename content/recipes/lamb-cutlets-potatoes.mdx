Tender lamb cutlets pan-fried with thyme, served alongside oven-roasted tomatoes topped with goat cheese and herbes de Provence.

## Instructions

1. Fry the lamb cutlets in rapeseed oil or butter with thyme until cooked to your liking.
2. Slice the tomatoes and top with goat cheese slices.
3. Drizzle with oil and sprinkle with herbes de Provence.
4. Roast the tomatoes with goat cheese in the oven until the cheese is soft and golden.
5. Serve the lamb cutlets with the roasted potatoes and tomatoes.
