A quick and easy chili con carne using Heinz beans in tomato sauce, served over spaghetti for a hearty family dinner.

## Instructions

1. Brown the minced meat in a pot.
2. Add the chili con carne powder (Tex Mex spice mix) and stir.
3. Add the Heinz beans in tomato sauce and a little water.
4. Simmer until the flavors come together.
5. Cook the spaghetti according to package directions.
6. Serve the chili over the spaghetti.
