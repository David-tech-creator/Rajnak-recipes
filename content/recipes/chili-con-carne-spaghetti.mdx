---
title: Chili Con Carne with Spaghetti
date: '2022-01-26'
category: Family Recipes
image: /images/recipes/chili-con-carne-spaghetti.jpg
prepTime: '10 mins'
cookTime: '30 mins'
servings: 4
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 500 g beef mince
  - 1 large onion, finely chopped
  - 2 cloves garlic, minced
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 packet Tex-Mex chili con carne spice mix (or 1 tbsp paprika + 1 tsp cumin + ½ tsp chili powder)
  - 2 tins Heinz baked beans in tomato sauce (415 g each)
  - 2 dl water
  - 1 tsp salt, plus more to taste
  - Black pepper, to taste
  - 400 g spaghetti
  - 50 g grated cheese, to serve
  - 1 tbsp chopped parsley, to serve
instructions:
  - Heat the oil in a large pot over medium-high. Add the mince and brown well, breaking it up with a spoon, until no liquid is left.
  - Tip in the onion and garlic and soften for 4 minutes.
  - Stir in the chili spice mix and cook 30 seconds, until fragrant.
  - Add the Heinz beans straight from the tin along with 2 dl water. Season with salt and pepper.
  - Reduce the heat and simmer gently for 20 minutes, stirring now and then, until thick and rich.
  - Meanwhile, cook the spaghetti in plenty of salted boiling water until al dente. Drain.
  - Divide the spaghetti between four deep plates. Ladle the chili generously over the top.
  - Scatter with grated cheese and a little parsley.
---

A pantry-friendly weeknight chili over spaghetti — the Heinz beans are the lazy genius move. Use a proper Tex-Mex spice mix if you have one. Always with cheese.
