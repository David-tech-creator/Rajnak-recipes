A traditional Central European egg noodle dish made fresh with a spätzli maker. Light, tender dumplings perfect as a side dish.

## Instructions

1. Mix egg with flour and enough water so dough is neither too soft nor too hard.
2. Mix it right before cooking. Don't mix too much.
3. Use spätzli maker with holes placed above boiling water, let them drip through.
4. Stir with wooden spoon.
5. Ready when risen to top of water.
6. Drain, rinse with cold water.
7. Add to bowl with a bit of olive oil.
