  - 2 yellow onions

  - 5 cloves of garlic
  - 3 tablespoons of rapeseed oil
  - 1 red chili
  - 2 teaspoons cumin
  - 2 teaspoons smoked paprika powder
  - 500 g black lenses, beluga lenses or similar
  - 33 cl beer, optional variety
  - 1 dice vegetable broth
  - 800 g of crushed tomatoes
  - salt and pepper
  - 1 green pepper
  - 1 silver onion

  - nacho chips

  - coriander

1. Chop small onions, garlic, chili and celery. Take a large saucepan and add oil. Bake

onions, garlic, chilli and celery until it has a nice color, add paprika and cumin.

2. Then add lentils, beer, crushed tomatoes and vegetable broth. Salt and pepper. Let

it boil properly without lid to reduce the liquid. Let cook for about 10-15 minutes.

3. Chop green peppers and onion and mix in the casserole just before serving so they

are still crispy.

4. Serve with avocado, chips, coriander and sour cream!
