ingredients:
  - "Preparation: 15 min"
  - "Cooking: 20 min"
  - 1 bunch of white asparagus
  - 2 egg yolks
  - 30g plain flour
  - 2 cubes of bouillon
  - 12cl of freshly squeezed orange juice
  - 2 table spoons of creme fraiche
  - 40g butter
  - salt & pepper
prepTime: ''
cookTime: ''
servings: 4
featured: false
---

1. Bring 1.25 litres of water to boil in a pan with bouillon cubes crumbled

2. Remove the hard base part of the asparagus and peel them. Keep the

points and add the rods in the pan containing the bouillon. Cook them for

10 minutes, then add the points and continue cooking for 5 min.

3. Drain the asparagus while keeping the bouillon. Keep the points

4. Slowly melt butter in a pan. Powder with flour, mix for 1 min, then add 1

litre of bouillon little by little, while whisking. Add the asparagus rods and

let it simmer for 5 min on a low heat.

5. Lightly mix the soup. Beat the egg yolks with the cream and orange juice.

Add this mix to the pan and also add the previously kept points. Season it.

6. Serve it warm, make sure it’s no longer boiling.
