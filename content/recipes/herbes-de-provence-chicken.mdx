Simple oven-roasted chicken drumsticks seasoned with herbes de provence, served with fluffy rice.

## Instructions

Season chicken drumsticks with salt, oil and herbes de provence (basil, thyme, marjoram). Wrap in aluminum foil. Roast in oven until cooked through and golden. Serve with rice.
