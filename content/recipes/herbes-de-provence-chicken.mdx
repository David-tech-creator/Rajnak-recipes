title: Herbes de Provence Chicken Drumsticks with Rice

servings: 4
kitchenNote: 'Skin side down. Don''t move it.'
signoff: 'Bon appétit.'
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 8 chicken drumsticks
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 2 tsp salt
  - 1 tbsp herbes de Provence (basil, thyme, marjoram, rosemary)
  - 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  - 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  - 1 lemon, in wedges
  - 3 dl long-grain rice
  - 6 dl water
  - 1 tsp salt (for the rice)
  - 1 tbsp butter
instructions:
  - Heat the oven to 200°C.
  - Pat the drumsticks dry. Toss with olive oil, salt, herbes de Provence, pepper and crushed garlic, rubbing the mixture into the skin.
  - Wrap the drumsticks in aluminium foil in a parcel (or arrange in a foil-covered baking dish). Roast for 30 minutes.
  - Open the foil, raise the heat to 220°C, and roast another 10–15 minutes uncovered until the skin is crisp and golden.
  - While the chicken roasts, rinse the rice. Bring the water to a boil with salt and butter, add the rice, cover, and simmer on low for 12 minutes. Rest off the heat 5 minutes, then fluff with a fork.
  - Serve the drumsticks on a bed of rice with a wedge of lemon to squeeze over.
---

A no-fuss Provençal weeknight dinner — wrapped in foil for the first half so the meat steams tender, then unwrapped to get the skin properly crisp.
