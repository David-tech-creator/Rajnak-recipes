1. Boil the rice according to the instructions on the package.

2. Cut the sausage into sticks. Peel and chop the onion.

3. Fry sausage and onion in the oil in a frying pan for about 5 minutes. Add

tomato puree and fry for a few minutes.

Open timer: 5 minutes

4. Stir in cream, milk, soy and Dijon mustard. Let simmer for about 5

minutes.

Open timer: 5 minutes

5. Taste with pepper and possibly salt.

To serve: Serve rice and cabbage salad on the side.

Tip: If you want the sauce a little thicker, rub it with cornstarch or wheat

flour mixed in a little water.
