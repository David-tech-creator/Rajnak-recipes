title: Sausage Stroganoff with Rice (Korvstroganoff)

prepTime: '10 mins'
cookTime: '20 mins'
servings: 4
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 4 portions basmati or long-grain rice
  - 550 g falukorv (Swedish sausage)
  - 1 large yellow onion, chopped
  - 1 tbsp neutral oil
  - 3 tbsp tomato paste
  - 2.5 dl single cream
  - 1 dl whole milk
  - 1 tbsp Japanese soy sauce
  - 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  - Black pepper, to taste
  - Salt, to taste
  - 'To serve:'
  - Coarsely chopped white cabbage, dressed with parsley and lemon
instructions:
  - Cook the rice according to the packet, then keep warm under a clean towel.
  - Peel and chop the onion. Cut the falukorv into thick batons.
  - Heat the oil in a wide frying pan over medium-high heat. Fry the sausage and onion for about five minutes until lightly coloured.
  - Stir in the tomato paste and fry for a further two minutes until darker and fragrant.
  - Pour in the cream and milk, then add the soy and mustard. Simmer gently for five minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
  - Taste, season with pepper and salt if needed, and serve over the rice with the cabbage salad on the side.
---

The Swedish midweek workhorse — pink falukorv sausage in a tangy tomato-cream sauce over rice. Twenty minutes start to finish, and there's always a child who asks for seconds.
