Generously spiced Hungarian-style meatballs with red pepper powder and marjoram, rolled in breadcrumbs and fried until golden. Makes a large batch perfect for feeding a crowd.

## Instructions

1. Roast the onion in oil until it gets color.
2. Add 2 tablespoons of red pepper powder to the onion.
3. Put in the minced meat.
4. Mix all ingredients evenly with your hands: minced meat, onion mixture, garlic, salt, pepper, dissolved broth cubes, eggs, marjoram, and the soaked baguette.
5. Roll into small balls.
6. Roll each ball in breadcrumbs.
7. Fry in rapeseed oil until golden and cooked through.
