title: Coconut Chocolate Balls (Kokosbollar / Chokladbollar)

servings: 20
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 100 g butter, very soft
  - 1 dl caster sugar
  - 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  - 2 tsp vanilla sugar
  - 3 dl rolled oats
  - 2 tbsp cold strong coffee (or cold water)
  - Pinch of salt
  - 'To coat:'
  - 1 dl desiccated coconut (or pearl sugar)
instructions:
  - In a bowl, beat the soft butter with the sugar, cocoa, vanilla sugar, and salt until smooth and creamy.
  - Stir in the oats and the cold coffee. Mix thoroughly with a spoon, then with your hands, until the mixture holds together.
  - Cover and chill for ten minutes to firm up.
  - Roll the mixture into 20 walnut-sized balls between damp palms.
  - Spread the desiccated coconut on a small plate and roll each ball to coat fully.
  - Place on a tray and chill in the fridge for at least thirty minutes before serving.
---

The classic Swedish no-bake fika treat — soft, fudgy, full of oats, rolled in snowy coconut. Kid-friendly to make and faster than waiting for the kettle.
