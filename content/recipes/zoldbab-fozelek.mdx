---
title: Zöldbab Főzelék
date: '2022-03-08'
category: Family Recipes
image: /images/recipes/zoldbab-fozelek.jpg
prepTime: '10 mins'
cookTime: '25 mins'
servings: 4
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 800 g green beans, trimmed
  - 3 small yellow onions (or 1 large), finely chopped
  - 1 dl sunflower or neutral oil
  - 2 tbsp plain flour
  - 1 tbsp sweet Hungarian paprika
  - 5 dl water
  - 200 g Spanish or Polish red sausage (cabanossi, chorizo), sliced
  - 1 tsp salt, plus more to taste
  - Black pepper, to taste
  - 2 tbsp sour cream, to serve (optional)
instructions:
  - Heat the oil in a wide pot and brown the chopped onions over medium heat until deeply golden, 8 to 10 minutes.
  - Add the flour off the heat and stir until smooth — a quick blond roux. When it starts to turn light brown, take it off the heat and stir in the paprika so it blooms without burning.
  - Pour in the water in a steady stream while whisking, returning to the heat as you go, so there are no lumps.
  - Add the green beans and a generous pinch of salt. Cover and simmer over medium-low heat (about 6 out of 10 on the dial) for 15 minutes.
  - Add the sliced sausage and cook another 10 minutes, until the beans are tender and the sauce is silky.
  - Taste for salt and pepper. Serve with a spoonful of sour cream stirred in at the table.
---

A classic Hungarian főzelék — somewhere between a stew and a thick vegetable side — built on a paprika roux. The smoked sausage at the end is what makes it dinner.
