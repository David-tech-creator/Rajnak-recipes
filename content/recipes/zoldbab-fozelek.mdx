A Hungarian green bean stew thickened with a paprika roux and served with sliced red sausage.

## Instructions

1. Chop onions, brown in hot oil.
2. Add flour. Pour off the heat.
3. When it starts to get a little brown, add tablespoon paprika powder.
4. Pour in water (half liter), mix so no lumps.
5. Pour in green beans.
6. Boil under lid (6/10 heat).
7. Add sausage slices.
8. Cook 10 minutes.
