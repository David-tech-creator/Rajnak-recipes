---
title: Banana Chocolate Cake
date: '2026-05-23'
category: Found Recipes
image: /images/recipes/banana-chocolate-cake.jpg
ingredients:
  - 125 g butter
  - 2 bananas - well ripe + 1
  - 0.5 dl sour cream - or Turkish yogurt
  - 3 eggs
  - 2.5 dl powdered sugar
  - 100 g chocolate - dark or light
  - 3 dl wheat flour - (180 g)
  - 1 dl cocoa
  - 1 krm salt
  - 2 tsp baking powder
prepTime: ''
cookTime: ''
servings: 4
featured: false
---

1. Set the oven to 200 °. Melt the butter. Line an oblong 1 1/2 liter mold with

parchment paper.

2. Peel and mash 2 bananas with the sour cream. Whisk eggs and sugar

lightly. Chop the chocolate. Mix flour, cocoa, salt and baking powder in a

bowl.

3. Pour melted butter and mashed bananas into the egg and sugar mixture.

Sift down the flour mixture. Mix well and turn the chocolate over.

4. Pour the batter into the mold. Peel and cut 1 banana into slices lengthwise

and place the slices on top of the mold. Bake in the lower part of the oven

for about 50 minutes. Feel with a knitting needle that the cake is ready.

Allow to cool for 10-15 minutes before lifting with the baking paper. Let the

cake cool on a wire rack.
