  - 2 tbsp soy sauce
  - 1/4 tsp tabasco
  - 2 chicken breast fillets
  - 1 small avocado
  - 1 tbsp lime juice
  - 1 tbsp chopped chives
  - 75 ml sour cream
  - salt and pepper
  - 1 red pepper
  - iceberg/cos lettuce or 1 bunch rocket
  - 8 mushrooms
  - 1 red onion
  - 4 tortillas about 20cm diameter
prepTime: 40 mins
cookTime: 15 mins
servings: 2

Chicken fajitas with avocado dip, sour cream, and fresh vegetables wrapped in warm tortillas.

## Instructions

1. Stir soy sauce and tabasco, marinate chicken fillets covered for 30 minutes. Turn once during this time.
2. Halve avocado, remove stone, scoop out flesh with lime juice. Mash with fork, mix together sour cream and chives. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Chop pepper and lettuce into strips, mushrooms into thick slices. Peel onion and cut into eighths.
4. Place marinated chicken breasts in solid cooking container. Add pepper, mushrooms and onion.
5. Wrap tortillas in transparent film. Put on wire rack. Steam at 100°C for 15 minutes.
6. Cut chicken into strips. Spread avocado dip over each tortilla. Add lettuce, mushrooms, pepper, chicken and onion. Put sour cream on top. Roll up and serve.
