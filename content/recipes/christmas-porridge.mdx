A traditional Swedish Christmas porridge, creamy and comforting, served during the holidays.

## Instructions

1. Add water and milk cold to a pot. Add rice.
2. Cook in microwave.
3. Once cooked, add lid and continue on low power. Keep cooking.
4. Stir from time to time.
5. If rice is soft and liquid has evaporated, add butter and sugar.
