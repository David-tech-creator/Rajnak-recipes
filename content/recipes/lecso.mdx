A traditional Hungarian pepper and tomato stew with sausages, rice and eggs.

## Instructions

Fry the onion in canola oil (barely covering the bottom) until it gets a little color. Pour in 1 tablespoon of paprika powder. Add a bowl of cut peppers (in rings). Add a little less tomato than paprika. Add 1dl of water. Mix so that the tomatoes boil and they will give their own liquid. Add tomato puree and a meat broth dice. Cook 1-2dl rice in microns separately. When the bell pepper has become soft and the tomatoes have boiled, put in 3-4 sliced sausages and the rice. Wait 5min after the rice and the sausages are in. Add 3-4 mixed eggs. Gently mix for a few minutes until it looks like a lecho.
