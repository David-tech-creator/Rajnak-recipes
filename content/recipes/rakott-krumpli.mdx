A classic Hungarian layered potato casserole with hard-boiled eggs, sausage and creme fraiche.

## Instructions

Boil potatoes with shell on - not too soft, but cooked. Cook eggs until hard-boiled. Lubricate and bread a deep oven shape. Cut potatoes, eggs, sausages into slices. Layer: Potatoes, eggs, sausage, creme fraiche. On top add creme fraiche. Put in the oven 225 degrees until it gets nice color.
