A layered gratin with pork tenderloin, mushroom cream sauce, and golden mashed potatoes.

## Instructions

Make mashed potatoes (a little firmer, not so soft). Cook potatoes until soft, add butter, salt and milk. Place tomato slices (4-5 medium-sized tomatoes) in a baking dish on the bottom. Place mashed potatoes around the edge of the oven shape. Roast pork fillet (700 to 800g) in butter (3 minutes per side or until it turns to strength five out of nine). Place the meat slices on top of the tomato layer, salt and pepper. In the same frying pan (after roasting the meat), fry mushrooms (300 to 400g) slices. When fried, pour half a liter (5dl) of cream into the frying pan together with the mushrooms. Cook together for a sauce. Pour in oven shape on top of meat. Cover with thin smoked ham slices and cheese slices. Place the mold in the oven 220 degrees until the potatoes turn color (about 20 minutes).
