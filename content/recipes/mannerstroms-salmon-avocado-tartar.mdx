ingredients:
  - 300g fresh salmon
  - Salt, pepper
  - 2 avocados
  - Rucola rocket salad
  - About 10 Chives sticks
  - 100g whitefish roe (löjrom) (optional)
  - "Dressing:"
  - 2 tablespoons olive oil
  - 1 teaspoon sugar
  - 1 teaspoon light Dijon mustard
  - 1 teaspoon soy sauce
prepTime: ''
cookTime: ''
servings: 4
featured: false
---

1. Blend together the dressing

2. Take out the avocado and cut it into big cubes. Slice the salmon into big

cubes.

3. Mix the cubes with half the dressing and taste with salt and pepper.

4. Shape the tartar mix in a round shape.

5. Mix the salad and chives with the remaining dressing and place it on

plates. Place the tartar in the middle of the salad bed, and top it with the

optional whitefish roe. Serve with toasts.

3

Inspired by: Mannerström Bok, Fisk och skaldjur för alla sinnen.
