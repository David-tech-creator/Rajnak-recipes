ingredients:
  - 300 g very fresh, sashimi-grade salmon, skinned
  - 2 ripe avocados
  - 100 g whitefish roe (löjrom), optional
  - 1 bag rocket (rucola)
  - About 10 chives, snipped
  - Salt, to taste
  - Black pepper, to taste
  - 'For the dressing:'
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 tsp caster sugar
  - 1 tsp Dijon mustard (mild)
  - 1 tsp light soy sauce
  - 'To serve:'
  - 8 small toasts, butter-fried
instructions:
  - Whisk the dressing ingredients in a small bowl until emulsified.
  - Cut the salmon into 1-cm cubes. Halve the avocados, scoop out the flesh, and cut into similar-sized cubes.
  - In a bowl, gently mix the salmon and avocado with half the dressing. Season with a little salt and pepper. Taste and adjust.
  - Set a small ring mould on each plate. Spoon the tartar in, press lightly, and lift the ring away to keep the shape.
  - Dress the rocket and chives with the remaining dressing and arrange around the tartar.
  - Top each tartar with a small spoon of löjrom and serve immediately with butter-fried toasts on the side.
