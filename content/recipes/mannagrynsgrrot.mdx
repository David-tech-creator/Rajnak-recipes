ingredients:
  - 8 dl whole milk
  - 8 tbsp semolina (mannagryn)
  - 1 tsp caster sugar
  - 0.5 tsp salt
  - 'To serve:'
  - Caster sugar and ground cinnamon
  - Cold milk
  - A pat of butter (optional)
  - A spoon of jam (lingonberry or raspberry)
instructions:
  - Pour the milk into a heavy saucepan and bring it gently to the boil, watching it doesn't catch.
  - As soon as it boils, lower the heat and whisk in the semolina in a steady stream with the sugar and salt.
  - Cook for 4–5 minutes, whisking constantly, until the porridge thickens. (Microwave version: heat the milk in a large jug 4 minutes, whisk in the semolina, then microwave in 1-minute bursts, whisking between, for about 4 minutes total.)
  - Off the heat, let the porridge rest covered for two minutes — it will continue to thicken.
  - Ladle into bowls.
  - Top with a sprinkle of cinnamon sugar, a splash of cold milk, a knob of butter if you like, and a spoon of jam.
---

The Swedish nursery breakfast — semolina cooked in milk until silky. Whisk constantly or it goes lumpy. Cinnamon-sugar and cold milk on top, jam on the side.
