title: Zucchini Filled with Meat & Feta

servings: 4

ingredients:
  - 4 medium zucchini
  - 1 tbsp olive oil, plus extra for rubbing
  - 1 yellow onion, finely chopped
  - 2 cloves garlic, minced
  - 500 g minced beef (or beef and lamb mix)
  - 1 can (400 g) crushed tomatoes
  - 2 tbsp tomato paste
  - 1 tsp sweet paprika
  - 1 tsp dried oregano
  - 1 meat stock cube, crumbled
  - 50 g Parmesan, finely grated
  - 150 g feta cheese, crumbled
  - Salt and black pepper, to taste
  - Green salad, to serve
instructions:
  - Heat the oven to 200 °C. Cut the zucchini in half lengthways and scoop out the seeds with a spoon to make boats. Rub the cut surfaces with a little olive oil and arrange in a baking dish.
  - Heat the olive oil in a wide frying pan over medium heat. Fry the chopped onion and garlic for 2 minutes, until softened, then add the minced meat and brown well, breaking it up.
  - Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, paprika, oregano, crumbled stock cube, salt, and pepper. Simmer 8 to 10 minutes until thick.
  - Grate Parmesan over the inside of each zucchini boat, then fill generously with the meat mixture.
  - Scatter the crumbled feta over the top and bake in the middle of the oven for 25 to 30 minutes, until the zucchini is soft and the cheese is golden.
  - Let rest 5 minutes before serving with a green salad and a sharp dressing alongside.
---

A weeknight dinner that uses up garden zucchini and feels more elegant than it is. The feta on top crisps as it bakes — that's the best part.
