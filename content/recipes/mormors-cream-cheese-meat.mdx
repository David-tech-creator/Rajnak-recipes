A beloved family recipe passed down from Mormor -- tender pork fillets layered with rice and topped with a golden creme fraiche crust.

## Instructions

1. Boil rice with crumbled broth cubes.
2. Divide meat into 7-8mm thick slices.
3. Beat with meat hammer.
4. Salt all slices.
5. Sear slices on high heat until color.
6. Place meat in 1-2 low glass oven molds. Pepper.
7. Cut onion into rings, place on top of meat.
8. Cover with cooked rice. Salt more.
9. Smear sour cream on top.
10. Oven at 220 degrees for 15-20 minutes until color on top.
