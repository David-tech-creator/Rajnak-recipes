A traditional festive salad served at Christmas and Easter in the Rajnak family. A rich mix of vegetables, eggs, and apples bound together with homemade mayonnaise.

## Instructions

1. Boil the potatoes and eggs. Let them cool. Peel and dice.
2. Cook the peas and carrots. Let cool.
3. Peel the green apples and divide into small pieces.
4. Dice the pickled cucumber/gherkins.
5. Dice the cheese.
6. Add all ingredients in a large bowl. Crumble in the potatoes. Salt.
7. Put in the peas and carrots. Wait until everything cools.

## Homemade Mayonnaise

1. Mix 1 egg with 1 teaspoon lemon juice and 2.5 dl oil slowly until thick.
2. Add 1.5 dl creme fraiche and mustard.
3. Season with more salt and pepper to taste.
4. Fold the mayonnaise into the salad and mix gently.
