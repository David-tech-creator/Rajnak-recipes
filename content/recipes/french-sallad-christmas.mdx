ingredients:
  - 400 g frozen peas and carrots mix
  - 600 g waxy potatoes
  - 6 eggs
  - 4 pickled cucumbers (mild), diced
  - 2 green apples, peeled and diced
  - 200 g Emmentaler cheese, diced
  - 1 egg yolk (for mayonnaise)
  - 1 tsp lemon juice
  - 2.5 dl neutral oil (sunflower or rapeseed)
  - 1.5 dl crème fraîche
  - 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  - Salt and white pepper, to taste
instructions:
  - Boil the potatoes in salted water until tender, about 20 minutes. Drain and cool. Peel and dice into 1-cm cubes.
  - Hard-boil the eggs (9 minutes), cool in cold water, peel, and dice.
  - Cook the peas and carrots in lightly salted water for 4 minutes. Drain and cool.
  - In a large bowl, combine the potatoes, eggs, apples, pickled cucumbers, cheese, peas and carrots. Season lightly with salt.
  - For the mayonnaise, whisk the egg yolk with the lemon juice. Drizzle in the oil very slowly, whisking constantly, until thick and pale. Fold in the crème fraîche and mustard. Season with salt and pepper.
  - Fold the mayonnaise gently into the salad. Cover and chill at least 2 hours before serving so the flavours marry.
---

The family Christmas table wouldn't be complete without this — peas and apples and gherkins all bound in a homemade mayonnaise. Better the next day.
