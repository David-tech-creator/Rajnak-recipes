A classic Hungarian family dish of paprika-spiced meat and rice cooked together in a pressure cooker. Served with sauerkraut-filled peppers or gherkins.

## Instructions

1. Brown onion in oil in pressure cooker. Fry until golden (6-7 heat).
2. Pour in paprika powder. Remove from heat and mix.
3. Return to stove, add meat pieces and salt. Add 1dl water. Mix.
4. Separately prepare rice in frying pan with oil, heat until oily and slightly colored.
5. Pour rice into pressure cooker with 1.2-1.5 liters water.
6. Crumble in meat broth cubes. Add optional tomato/pepper.
7. Close pressure cooker, max heat, wait for 2 rings, lower to 3-4 strength.
8. Cook 20 minutes.
9. Serve with sauerkraut filled yellow peppers, gherkins, or Hungarian cucumber salad.
