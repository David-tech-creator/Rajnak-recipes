A creamy pasta dish combining tender chicken pieces with sundried tomatoes and fresh spinach in a rich cream sauce. A family favorite that comes together quickly.

## Instructions

1. Cut the chicken into small pieces. Cut the sundried tomatoes.
2. Roast the chicken in butter or oil. When it gets color, add salt and pepper.
3. When the chicken is halfway done, add the sundried tomatoes and leave for a few minutes.
4. Pour in 3-5 dl cream and let it boil.
5. Crumble in the chicken broth dice and stir.
6. Place in the cleaned spinach leaves and wait for them to wrinkle and shrink.
7. Serve with cooked tagliatelle.
