---
title: Lemon Tuna Cucumber Sandwich
date: '2022-04-04'
category: Quick & Easy
image: /images/recipes/lemon-tuna-sandwich.jpg
prepTime: '10 mins'
cookTime: '0 mins'
servings: 2
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 1 tin (about 160 g) tuna in olive oil, well-drained
  - 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  - 1 tbsp crème fraîche
  - 1 tbsp lemon juice
  - Zest of 0.5 lemon
  - 2 tbsp chives, finely snipped
  - 0.5 small red onion, finely chopped
  - Salt, to taste
  - Black pepper, to taste
  - 4 slices good sourdough or rye bread
  - 0.5 cucumber, sliced thin
  - A handful rocket or baby spinach
instructions:
  - In a bowl, flake the drained tuna with a fork. Add the mayonnaise, crème fraîche, lemon juice and zest, chives, and red onion.
  - Stir to combine and season with salt and pepper. Taste and add more lemon if you want more brightness.
  - Lightly toast the bread on both sides.
  - Lay the cucumber slices on two pieces of toast in an even layer, then top with the rocket or spinach.
  - Pile the tuna mix generously on top and finish with a crack of pepper.
  - Close the sandwiches with the second slice of toast, press down lightly, and cut on the diagonal.
---

The clean, sharp tuna sandwich — lemon and chives carry the day, cucumber keeps it cool. The summer lunch you fall back on between something more ambitious.
