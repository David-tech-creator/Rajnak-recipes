A quick and flavorful family meal -- crispy chili chicken served with rice and a creamy avocado sauce.

## Instructions

1. Fry chicken in oil with salt and chili sauce until cooked through and golden.
2. Cook rice according to package instructions.
3. For the avocado sauce: mash avocado and mix with creme fraiche, salt, and pepper.
4. Serve chicken over rice with avocado sauce on the side.
