  - up leftover veggies in the fridge.
  - Ingredients
  - 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  - 1 tablespoon crushed garlic
  - 1 egg
  - 125g (4oz) sirloin steak, thinly sliced
  - 150g (5oz) thick, flat fresh rice noodles (or dry noodles that have been
  - soaked)
  - 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  - 1 tablespoon dark soy sauce
  - Pinch of white pepper
  - 1/2 tablespoon fish sauce (optional)
  - 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
  - Large handful of Chinese kale, roughly chopped (or any leafy greens you
  - can find)
  - Handful of beansprouts (optional)

1. Heat the oil in a wok over a medium heat. When hot stir fry the garlic

until fragrant.

2. Add the beef and stir fry until the meat is cooked through. Followed by

the egg.

3. Add noodles and stir well to combine. Mix in the oyster sauce and soy

sauce and stir fry for 1 minute. Add the rest of the seasoning.

4. Lastly, add the kale and stir fry for a few moments making sure everything

is combined. Now it’s ready to serve!

5. Garnish with a bit more white pepper before serving.
