A quick and easy chicken curry with coconut milk served over rice.

## Instructions

1. Prepare rice in advance.
2. Cut chicken into cubes.
3. Fry in olive oil until color gets cooked through. Fry with a tablespoon of olive oil or more.
4. Add 1/3 of the curry. Mix for 3-5 minutes.
5. Pour in coconut milk (a packet). Mix. Cook.
6. When chicken is fried, pour the whole sauce on the chicken. Boil it together. Finished.
