---
title: Tuna, Feta, Tomato, Avocado & Quinoa Salad
date: '2026-05-23'
category: Quick & Easy
image: /images/recipes/tuna-feta-tomato-quinoa-salad.jpg
prepTime: '15 mins'
cookTime: '15 mins'
servings: 2
kitchenNote: Rinse the quinoa or it stays bitter.
signoff: Tuck in.
difficulty: Easy
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 1.5 dl quinoa, rinsed
  - 3 dl water
  - 1 can good tuna in olive oil (about 160 g), drained
  - 150 g feta cheese, crumbled
  - 200 g cherry tomatoes, halved
  - 1 ripe avocado, diced
  - 0.5 small red onion, finely chopped
  - 1 small handful fresh parsley or mint, chopped
  - 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 1 tbsp lemon juice
  - 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  - Salt and black pepper, to taste
instructions:
  - Bring the quinoa and water to a boil with a pinch of salt, lower the heat, cover, and simmer for 12 minutes until the water is absorbed. Fluff with a fork and let cool.
  - Whisk the olive oil, lemon juice, mustard, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl.
  - Add the cooled quinoa to the dressing and toss to coat. Fold in the tomatoes, red onion, and herbs.
  - Just before serving, top with the flaked tuna, crumbled feta, and diced avocado. Toss gently or serve in layers.
---

A bright, no-cook-after-the-quinoa lunch — packs well for the office or a picnic. Add the avocado at the last minute so it stays green.
