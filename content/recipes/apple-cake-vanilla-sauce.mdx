title: Apple Cake with Vanilla Sauce
date: '2026-05-23'

prepTime: '20 mins'
cookTime: '30 mins'
servings: 4
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 5 apples (Granny Smith or other firm tart variety)
  - 100 g butter, softened
  - 6 tbsp brown sugar
  - 1 sheet all-butter puff pastry, thawed
  - 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  - 1 vanilla pod, split, or 1 tsp vanilla extract
  - 1 tbsp lemon juice
  - "For the vanilla sauce:"
  - 3 dl whole milk
  - 1 dl double cream
  - 3 egg yolks
  - 3 tbsp caster sugar
  - 1 vanilla pod, split and scraped
instructions:
  - Heat the oven to 180°C. Peel, core, and slice the apples thin. Toss with lemon juice, cinnamon, and 2 tbsp of the brown sugar.
  - Smear the soft butter generously over the base of a 24-cm round tart tin. Scatter the remaining 4 tbsp brown sugar evenly over the butter — it will caramelise as the tart bakes.
  - Arrange the apple slices on the buttered sugar in tight, overlapping circles.
  - Roll the puff pastry to fit, lay it over the apples, and tuck the edges down inside the tin.
  - Bake for 30 minutes, until the pastry is deeply golden and the sugar bubbles around the edge. Let it rest five minutes, then invert onto a serving plate.
  - For the sauce, warm the milk, cream, and split vanilla pod until just steaming. Whisk the yolks with the sugar, pour the warm milk in slowly, then return everything to the pan and stir over low heat until it coats a spoon.
  - Serve the warm tart with the vanilla sauce poured generously over each slice.
---

A French-Swiss tarte tatin style apple cake, inspired by Dominique Gauthier. Use firm tart apples so they hold their shape and balance the caramel. Best eaten warm, the same day.
