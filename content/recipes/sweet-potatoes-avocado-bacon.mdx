title: Sweet Potatoes with Avocado & Bacon

difficulty: Easy
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 2 medium sweet potatoes, scrubbed
  - 200 g streaky bacon
  - 1 ripe avocado
  - 2 tbsp soured cream or Greek yogurt
  - 1 tbsp lime juice
  - 1 spring onion, thinly sliced
  - 1 small handful fresh coriander
  - 1 small red chili, finely chopped, optional
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - Salt and black pepper
instructions:
  - Heat the oven to 200 °C. Prick the sweet potatoes all over with a fork, rub with the olive oil and a pinch of salt, and bake on a tray for 45 minutes, until completely soft when squeezed.
  - About 15 minutes before the potatoes are done, lay the bacon on a second tray and bake until deep golden and crisp. Drain on paper and crumble into shards.
  - Mash the avocado in a bowl with the lime juice, half the spring onion, a pinch of salt, and the chili if using.
  - Split the hot sweet potatoes open with a knife, push the ends together to fluff the flesh, and season with salt and pepper. Spoon the avocado over, top with the crispy bacon, a dollop of soured cream, the remaining spring onion, and a scatter of coriander.
---

A perfect lazy weeknight dinner — and surprisingly elegant on the plate. The contrast of soft sweet potato, cool avocado, and salty bacon is the whole point.
