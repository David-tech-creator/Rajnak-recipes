A refreshing and colorful salad combining pear, avocado, and cottage cheese — affectionately called the "Fun" Salad in the Rajnak family.

## Instructions

Chop iceberg lettuce and arrange on a platter. Add sliced pear, avocado, red onion, and black olives. Top with cottage cheese and drizzle with salad dressing.
