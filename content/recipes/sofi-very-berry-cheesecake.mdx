ingredients:
  - 250 g fresh raspberries and blueberries (mixed)
  - 250 g Philadelphia cream cheese
  - 1 dl whipping cream
  - 3 tbsp icing sugar
  - 2 tbsp caster sugar
  - 0.5 tsp vanilla powder
  - 6 digestive biscuits
  - 30 g unsalted butter, melted
instructions:
  - Put 125 g of the berries in a small saucepan with the caster sugar and vanilla powder. Bring to a gentle simmer and cook for 3 minutes, until the berries break down. Cool completely.
  - Whip the cream cheese, icing sugar, and whipping cream together until thick, fluffy, and holding soft peaks.
  - Crush the digestive biscuits into coarse crumbs in a bag with a rolling pin. Stir the melted butter through the crumbs.
  - Layer in glasses or small bowls: biscuit crumbs at the bottom, then cream cheese mixture, then a spoonful of the cooled berry compote, then a few fresh berries.
  - Repeat the layers if you have room, finishing with whole fresh berries on top.
  - Chill for at least 30 minutes before eating. Voilà!

Sofi Fahrman's no-bake cheesecake-in-a-glass — quick, pretty, and a real crowd-pleaser. Makes two large portions or four small. The berry compote at the bottom turns into a jammy surprise.
