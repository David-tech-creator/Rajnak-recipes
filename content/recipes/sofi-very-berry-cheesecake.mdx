  - Quick and easy cheese cake!
  - "Ingredients:"
  - (2 large portions or 4 small.)
  - 250 g fresh raspberries and blueberries
  - 250 g Philadelphia cream cheese
  - 1 dl whipping cream
  - 3 tablespoons icing sugar
  - 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  - ½ teaspoon vanilla powder
  - 6 pcs Digestive biscuits
  - 30 g butter
prepTime: ''
cookTime: ''
servings: 4
featured: false
---

1. Boil 125 g of the berries together with powdered sugar and vanilla powder,

which you then let cool.

2. Whisk Philadelphia cheese, icing sugar and cream to a fluffy cream.

3. Crush the Digestive Biscuits, melt the butter and mix.

4. Now just bring together the raspberries, blueberries, cream, berry

compote and biscuits. Voilà!
