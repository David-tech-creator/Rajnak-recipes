A hearty Hungarian gulasch soup passed down from Mamma. Rich with beef, vegetables, and paprika, finished with csipet tarhonya for an authentic touch.

## Instructions

1. Cut the meat into small pieces (2cm x 2cm). Chop the onions.
2. Let the onions fry in oil until they get color.
3. Sprinkle in the paprika powder and stir.
4. Add the meat straight after and stir.
5. Add salt.
6. Add the water, crumble the bouillon, add vegetables and potatoes.
7. Simmer with the lid on for about an hour until the meat becomes soft. Alternatively, use a pressure cooker for 30-40 minutes.
8. After opening, add the csipet tarhonya.
9. Finish with fresh parsley.
