---
title: Toast Skagen
date: '2023-09-15'
category: Family Recipes
ingredients:
  - 4 slices of white bread
  - 200g shrimp, peeled
  - 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  - 1 tbsp sour cream
  - 1 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
  - 1 tsp lemon juice
  - Salt and white pepper
  - Butter for frying
  - Dill and lemon for garnish
  - Fish roe (optional)
instructions:
  - Cut bread into rounds and fry in butter until golden.
  - Mix shrimp, mayonnaise, sour cream, dill, and lemon juice.
  - Season with salt and white pepper.
  - Top each bread round with the shrimp mixture.
  - Garnish with dill, lemon, and fish roe if using.
prepTime: 15 mins
cookTime: 5 mins
servings: 4
---

Toast Skagen is a classic Swedish appetizer named after a fishing port in Denmark. It's elegant yet simple, making it perfect for parties and gatherings.

## Serving Suggestion

This dish is traditionally served as a starter at formal dinners, but it also works well as a light lunch or part of a buffet spread.
