title: Toast Skagen

cookTime: '5 mins'
servings: 4
kitchenNote: Fry the bread in butter. Not toast.
signoff: Smaklig måltid.
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 4 slices white country bread, crusts off
  - 50 g butter, for frying
  - 400 g cold-water shrimp, peeled (skalade räkor)
  - 3 tbsp good mayonnaise
  - 2 tbsp crème fraîche or sour cream
  - 2 tbsp fresh dill, finely chopped, plus sprigs to garnish
  - 1 tsp lemon juice
  - 1 small shallot, very finely chopped
  - 50 g löjrom (vendace roe), to top
  - 1 lemon, cut into wedges
  - Salt and white pepper
instructions:
  - Cut the bread into neat squares or rounds. Fry in butter over medium heat until deep golden and crisp on both sides. Drain on paper.
  - Stir together the shrimp, mayonnaise, crème fraîche, chopped dill, lemon juice, and shallot in a bowl. Season carefully with salt and white pepper — taste before adding more, as the shrimp are already salty.
  - Pile the shrimp mixture generously on top of each toast.
  - Crown each with a heaped teaspoon of löjrom, a sprig of dill, and serve with a lemon wedge alongside.
---

The Swedish appetizer designed by Tore Wretman in the 1950s, named after the Danish fishing port. The crisp buttery toast under the cold creamy shrimp and the orange jewel of löjrom on top is the whole point.
