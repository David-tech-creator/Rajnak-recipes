  - Lemon and poppy seed cake is a classic American combination, and olive
  - oil gives the cake a slightly more adult taste. At the same time, the cream
  - cheese frosting makes it guaranteed to go home with the children. The
  - recipe below gives a cake of about 10 - 12 pieces. Feel free to make it in a
  - normal shape of about 20 centimeters in diameter.
  - "Ingredients:"
  - 2 eggs
  - 1 1/2 dl granulated sugar
  - Grated peel from 2 lemons and 1 orange
  - 1/2 dl pressed orange
  - 150 g ricotta (or quark or kesella)
  - 1 dl olive oil
  - 2 1/2 dl wheat flour
  - 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  - 1/2 dl blue poppy seeds
  - Butter and breadcrumbs to the mold
  - Set the oven to 175 degrees. Whisk eggs and sugar white and fluffy. Stir in
  - grated lemon and orange peel. Mix the squeezed orange with the ricotta,
  - and then add the olive oil in a thin stream. Stir the ricotta mixture into
  - the egg batter and then mix in the flour, baking powder and poppy seeds
  - separately and turn into the batter. Pour the batter into a greased and
  - breaded form. Bake for 20-30 minutes in the middle of the oven. Decorate
  - with icing or our delicious cream cheese frosting.
  - "Delicious Cream Cheese-frosting :"
  - 25 g room temperature butter
  - 2 1/2 dl icing sugar
  - 1/2 teaspoon vanilla sugar
  - 1 teaspoon squeezed lemon and a little grated peel
  - 50 g Philadelphia cream cheese or similar cream cheese
  - about 1 tablespoon poppy seeds
  - Whisk together all the ingredients (except the poppy seeds and lemon zest,
  - which you are going to decorate with) to a smooth frosting. Spread on the
  - cake when it has cooled and decorate with extra poppy seeds and grated
  - lemon peel! NOTE, it is of course possible to “freestyle” with the frosting .
  - For example, if you have no butter, just run with Cream cheese.
