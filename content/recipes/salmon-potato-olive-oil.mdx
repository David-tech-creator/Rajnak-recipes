title: Salmon with Potato, Red Onions and Zucchini in Olive Oil
date: '2022-04-12'

prepTime: '15 mins'
cookTime: '35 mins'
servings: 4
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 4 salmon fillets (about 150 g each), skin on
  - 800 g small waxy potatoes, halved
  - 2 red onions, cut into wedges
  - 2 medium zucchini, sliced 1 cm thick
  - 1 dl extra-virgin olive oil
  - 4 sprigs fresh thyme
  - 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  - 1 lemon, sliced
  - Flaky sea salt and black pepper, to taste
  - A small handful flat-leaf parsley, chopped, to serve
instructions:
  - Heat the oven to 200 °C.
  - Toss the halved potatoes with half the olive oil, the crushed garlic, two sprigs of thyme, and a good pinch of sea salt. Spread on a large baking tray and roast for 15 minutes.
  - Add the red onion wedges and zucchini to the tray. Drizzle with another tablespoon of oil and turn gently. Roast for another 10 minutes.
  - Pat the salmon dry, season with salt and pepper, and nestle the fillets skin-side up onto the vegetables. Drape over a few lemon slices and the remaining thyme. Drizzle with the rest of the olive oil.
  - Return the tray to the oven and roast for 10–12 minutes, until the salmon flakes easily.
  - Scatter with chopped parsley and bring the whole tray to the table.
---

One-tray summer dinner: salmon roasted on a bed of sweet onions, zucchini and crisp-edged potatoes, slicked with peppery olive oil. Eat with whatever cold white wine is open.
