A hearty Hungarian sauerkraut stew with tender meat and smoked sausage, served with a generous dollop of sour cream.

## Instructions

1. Chop onion, fry in oil.
2. Add paprika powder, mix.
3. Add meat, mix until colored for one minute.
4. Add sauerkraut (squeezed). Mix.
5. Pour water until almost covers it.
6. Crumble in 3 broth cubes.
7. Add smoked sausage.
8. Boil in pressure cooker on max heat, wait for 2 rings, then lower to 3-4, cook 20-30 minutes.
9. Eat with sour cream / creme fraiche.
