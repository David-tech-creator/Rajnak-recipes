A simple and classic Italian-inspired salad of ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and fragrant basil drizzled with olive oil.

## Instructions

Slice tomatoes and fresh mozzarella. Arrange on a plate, alternating slices. Top with fresh basil leaves. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt.
