title: Tomato & Mozzarella Salad with Basil
date: '2022-02-12'
category: Found Recipes
image: /images/recipes/tomato-mozzarella-basil.jpg
prepTime: '10 mins'
cookTime: '0 mins'
servings: 4
kitchenNote: Tomatoes at room temperature. Never the fridge.
signoff: Buon appetito.
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 4 large ripe tomatoes, room temperature
  - 2 balls fresh mozzarella di bufala (about 250 g each)
  - 1 large handful fresh basil leaves
  - 4 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 1 tbsp aged balsamic vinegar, optional
  - Flaky sea salt
  - Coarsely ground black pepper
instructions:
  - Slice the tomatoes about 1 cm thick. Tear or slice the mozzarella into similar-sized pieces.
  - Arrange tomato and mozzarella alternately on a large platter, slightly overlapping.
  - Tuck the basil leaves between the slices, larger ones torn in half.
  - Drizzle generously with olive oil and, if using, a few drops of balsamic. Finish with flaky salt and pepper just before serving.
---

The Italian summer plate that needs nothing — but only works with truly ripe tomatoes and fresh mozzarella at room temperature. Don't refrigerate the tomatoes.
