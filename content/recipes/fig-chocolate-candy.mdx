ingredients:
  - 200g grounded almonds
  - 50g sugar
  - 1/2 dl cream
  - 1dl cognac
  - 250g figues
  - 200-300g chocolate (Cailler Dessert Noir 64%)
prepTime: 30 mins
cookTime: 15 mins
servings: 45
featured: false
---

Elegant homemade chocolate truffles with figs, almonds, and cognac. Makes approximately 45 balls -- perfect for holidays and special occasions.

## Instructions

1. Remove stems from figues, cut into smaller pieces.
2. Place all ingredients in mixer until paste.
3. Roll small balls.
4. Melt dark chocolate in bain-marie.
5. Roll balls to cover in chocolate.
6. Refrigerate until set.
