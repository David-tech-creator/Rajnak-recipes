---
title: Fig Chocolate Candy (Fikongodis)
date: '2022-03-04'
category: Family Recipes
image: /images/recipes/fig-chocolate-candy.jpg
prepTime: '30 mins'
cookTime: '15 mins'
servings: 45
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 200 g ground almonds
  - 50 g sugar
  - 0.5 dl double cream
  - 1 dl cognac
  - 250 g dried figs
  - 250 g dark chocolate (Cailler Dessert Noir 64% or similar)
instructions:
  - Remove the stems from the figs and cut them into smaller pieces.
  - Place the figs, ground almonds, sugar, cream and cognac in a food processor and blend until you have a thick, sticky paste.
  - Roll the paste into small balls, about the size of a hazelnut, and arrange on a tray lined with baking paper.
  - Melt the dark chocolate gently in a bain-marie, stirring until smooth.
  - Using a fork or dipping tool, roll each ball in the melted chocolate until fully coated. Return to the paper.
  - Refrigerate until set, at least one hour, then store in a tin between sheets of baking paper.
---

A grown-up Christmas sweet — fudgy, boozy, and one is never enough. Makes about 45 balls, perfect to wrap up as a gift.
