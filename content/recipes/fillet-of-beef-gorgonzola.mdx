A classic family favourite - tender beef fillet served with a rich, creamy gorgonzola sauce.

## Beef

Cut the tenderloin into 3-3.5 cm thick slices. Add some butter to the pan, heat the butter to strength 8, until it starts to turn brown. Add the meat, reduce the strength to 4 or 5, fry 3 mins on each side.

## Gorgonzola Sauce

Use the butter in which you roast the beef fillet. If necessary add one more click of butter. Crumble in 200g gorgonzola. Add 4 dl creme fraiche. Crumble a meat broth dice. A little soy, for the sauce to get color. Season with salt and pepper. Mix until the cheese melts (medium heat) and until it becomes a warm creamy sauce.
