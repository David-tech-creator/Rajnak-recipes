---
title: Fillet of Beef with Gorgonzola Sauce
date: '2022-01-01'
category: Family Recipes
image: /images/recipes/fillet-of-beef-gorgonzola.jpg
prepTime: '15 mins'
cookTime: '20 mins'
servings: 4
featured: true
ingredients:
  - 4 beef tenderloin steaks, 3–3.5 cm thick (about 200 g each)
  - 3 tbsp butter
  - 200 g gorgonzola
  - 4 dl crème fraîche
  - 1 beef stock cube
  - 1 tsp soy sauce
  - Salt and black pepper, to taste
instructions:
  - Take the steaks out of the fridge 30 minutes before cooking to come up to room temperature. Season generously with salt and pepper.
  - Heat the butter in a heavy pan over high heat (about strength 8) until it just starts to turn brown.
  - Lay in the steaks, then reduce the heat to medium (strength 4–5). Fry 3 minutes per side for medium-rare. Move to a warm plate, tent loosely, and rest while you make the sauce.
  - Using the same pan and its butter (add a knob more if it looks dry), crumble in the gorgonzola.
  - Pour in the crème fraîche, crumble the stock cube, and add the soy sauce for colour.
  - Stir over medium heat until the cheese melts into a warm, creamy sauce. Adjust salt and pepper.
  - Plate the rested steaks and spoon the sauce generously over the top.
---

A family classic for proper Saturday dinners — the gorgonzola and crème fraîche melt into a sauce that feels far more elaborate than it is. Serve with mashed potatoes and a green salad.
