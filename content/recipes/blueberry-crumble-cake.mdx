title: Blueberry Crumble Cake with Vanilla Ice Cream

ingredients:
  - 500g of blueberries (or raspberries)
  - 1 dl sugar (on the berries)
  - 2 limes (optional)
prepTime: ''
cookTime: ''
servings: 4
featured: false
---

2.25 dl flour

3dl oatmeal

150g room temperature butter

1.25dl sugar

1 teaspoon cardammon

Zest the lime and turn it around a bowl with berries and a bit of sugar.

Add the berries to a suitable oven shape.

Mix all ingredients in a bowl with hands until a solid but crumbly dough

has formed.

Taste the dough so its sweet enough.

Crumb in the dough over the berries and bake it at 180 degrees in the vapour

oven for around 20min (level 2 whole block).
