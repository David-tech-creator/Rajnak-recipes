Simple buttery potatoes finished with fresh parsley. A classic side dish that pairs perfectly with almost any main course.

## Instructions

1. Peel and cut the potatoes into pieces.
2. Boil in salted water until tender. Drain.
3. Toss with butter and fresh parsley before serving.
