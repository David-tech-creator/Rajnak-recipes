  - Pizza dough
  - Pizza tomato sauce

  - 200 grams of shredded fried kebab meat
  - 1 yellow onion
  - 3 pickled pepperonis
  - "Garlic sauce:"
  - 2 dl Greek natural yogurt
  - 1 squeezed clove of garlic
  - 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  - 1 krm salt
  - 1 cream black pepper
  - 1 teaspoon hot chili sauce to taste

1. Spread tomato sauce and pizza cheese on the pizza dough.

2. Add pizza toppings as desired - evenly distributed over the entire pizza.

For example, shredded onions, a few slices of kebab meat, a few slices of

tomato and pepperonis.

3. The time varies in different ovens. When the pizza has taken on color and

is thoroughly baked from below, it is ready.

4. Crumble over a little oregano, add if necessary. a few leaves of fresh basil

and possibly. sprinkle with finely grated parmesan on top before serving.

5.Garlicandchilisauce:Mix2dlGreekyogurtwithateaspoonofhomemade

hot chili sauce (or bought Sriracha) and a squeezed clove of garlic. Add a

tablespoon of lemon juice, a pinch of black pepper and a little salt. Spread

it on the pizza.
