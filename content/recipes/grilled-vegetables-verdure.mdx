ingredients:
  - 1 pepper, yellow or red
  - 1 large carrot
  - 1 eggplant (aubergine)
  - 1 zucchini
  - Dressing
  - 1 dl olive oil
  - 1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar or 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  - 2 tablespoons parsley, finely chopped
  - salt and pepper
  - Mix all the ingredients for the dressing in a small bowl and let stand.
  - Rinse and dry thoroughly peppers, eggplant and zucchini, peel the carrot.
  - Cut the peppers into wide strips and remove the seeds and the white inside.
  - Slice the remaining vegetables into thin slices about 3-4 mm, cut the carrot
  - at an angle so that the slices are larger.
  - Grill the vegetables for 7-10 minutes per side without oil
  - (it works great even in a grill pan).
  - Place the vegetables on a large flat plate and drizzle over the dressing.
  - "Variation: Sprinkle with salt and very thinly sliced ​​ garlic and drizzle over a"
  - little olive oil. Let stand and marinate for about half an hour before serving.
  - Of course you can choose other vegetables to grill. The important thing is
  - to grill the vegetables so that they are soft but at the same time al dente.
