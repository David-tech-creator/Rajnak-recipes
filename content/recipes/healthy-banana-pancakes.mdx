ingredients:
  - Banana pancakes
  - 2 bananas
  - 4 egg
  - 1 tsp vanilla powder
  - Salt
  - 2 tbsp butter - or rapeseed oil
  - Do this
  - Peel and mash the bananas with a fork in a bowl.
  - Crack the eggs over the bananas and mix to a batter, preferably with a
  - mixer. Add vanilla powder and salt.
  - Fry pancakes from the batter in butter or oil in a frying pan for about 3
  - minutes on each side. Feel free to use a plate lag with an easy-to-release
  - coating!
