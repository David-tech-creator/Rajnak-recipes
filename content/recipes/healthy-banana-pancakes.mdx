title: Healthy Banana Pancakes
date: '2026-05-23'

prepTime: '5 mins'
cookTime: '10 mins'
servings: 2
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 2 ripe bananas
  - 4 eggs
  - 1 tsp vanilla powder (or 1 tsp vanilla extract)
  - Pinch of salt
  - 2 tbsp butter (or rapeseed oil), for frying
  - Fresh berries, to serve
  - Maple syrup or honey, to serve
instructions:
  - Peel the bananas and mash them in a bowl with a fork until mostly smooth — a few small lumps are fine.
  - Crack in the eggs and whisk to a smooth batter, preferably with a hand mixer. Stir in the vanilla powder and a pinch of salt.
  - Heat half the butter in a non-stick frying pan over medium-low heat (these burn easily — go gentle).
  - Pour small puddles of batter into the pan, about 8 cm wide. Cook 2–3 minutes, until set on the bottom and bubbles appear on top.
  - Flip carefully — they're more delicate than regular pancakes — and cook 1–2 minutes on the second side.
  - Stack on warm plates and serve with fresh berries and a drizzle of maple syrup or honey.
---

The flour-free pancake breakfast — just bananas and eggs. The kids think it's a treat; the parents like that it's secretly healthy.
