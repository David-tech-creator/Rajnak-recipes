---
title: Homemade Ramen with Beef, Eggs & Pak Choi
date: '2026-05-23'
category: Found Recipes
image: /images/recipes/homemade-ramen-beef.jpg
ingredients:
  - 2 l of water
  - 1 vegetable bouillon cube
  - 1 red chili fruit
  - 4 cloves garlic
  - 150 g shiitake mushrooms (or other mushrooms)
  - 350 g silk tofu
  - 4 green onions
  - 180 g bean sprouts
  - 4 eggs
  - 3 tablespoons rapeseed oil
  - 6 tablespoons Japanese soy
  - 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  - 200 g egg noodles
  - 2 tbsp white misopaste
  - 100 g of spinach
  - black sesame seeds
  - Beef & pak choi
prepTime: ''
cookTime: ''
servings: 4
featured: false
---

1. Boil the water with the broth. Slice the chili and garlic and let it boil with

the broth for about 10 minutes.

2. Chop the green onions into nice, slightly larger pieces. Break the

mushroom into smaller pieces.

3. Put the eggs in boiling water and cook for exactly seven minutes and

rinse them in cold water.

4. Fry the mushrooms on high heat in the rapeseed oil until golden. Slice

the silk tofu into smaller cubes.

5. Strain the broth, pour it into a new saucepan and bring to the boil again.

Add the soy and sesame oil.

6. Add the noodles to the boiling broth and let them cook for four minutes.

7. Add the green onions, bean sprouts and remove from the heat. Add the

miso paste and stir. Add the tofu.

8. Divide the eggs in half Put the spinach in the bottom of the bowls and

pour the soup.

9. Top with eggs, fried mushrooms, and black sesame seeds.
