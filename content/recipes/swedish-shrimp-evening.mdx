---
title: Swedish Shrimp Evening (Räkafton)
date: '2026-05-23'
category: Family Recipes
image: /images/recipes/swedish-shrimp-evening.jpg
prepTime: '20 mins'
cookTime: '0 mins'
servings: 4
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 2 kg fresh whole shrimp in their shells, cooked
  - 1 large loaf of crusty bread or 12 slices toast
  - 250 g good salted butter
  - 200 g mayonnaise
  - 200 g aioli or skagenröra
  - 1 lemon, cut into wedges
  - 1 small bunch fresh dill
  - 1 hard-boiled egg per person, halved
  - Romaine lettuce leaves
  - Sea salt
  - Black pepper
  - Cold Swedish lager and snaps, to serve
instructions:
  - Pile the shrimp on a large platter set over crushed ice. Tuck lemon wedges and big fronds of dill around the edges.
  - Arrange the bread, butter, mayonnaise, aioli, eggs, and lettuce in bowls on the table so everyone can build their own open sandwich.
  - Each guest peels their own shrimp, layers lettuce, mayonnaise, and shrimp onto buttered bread, and finishes with dill, egg, sea salt, and a grind of pepper.
  - Serve with very cold lager and small glasses of snaps. Make sure there's a bowl on the table for shells.
---

A Swedish summer ritual: a mountain of cold shrimp, good bread, butter and mayonnaise, and a long evening of peeling and talking. Quantity matters — a kilo per couple is the right starting point.
