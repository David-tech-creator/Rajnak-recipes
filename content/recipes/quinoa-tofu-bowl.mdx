  - 4 dl quinoa
  - 2 dice vegetable broth
  - 550-600 g solid tofu (and/or silk)
  - 1 cup Japanese soy
  - 2 avocados
  - A few mini tomatoes
  - Hummus
  - Paprika powder
prepTime: ''
cookTime: ''
servings: 4

---

1. Cook the quinoa with the broth.

2. Dice tofu. Fry it on high heat in oil so that all sides get nice color. Place

it on a saucepan and pour over the soy. Allow to soak for at least 10 min.

3. Slice the avocado.

4. Mix in a bit of paprika powder in the hummus.

4. Put quinoa, tofu, avocado, and a click of hummus in a bowl.
