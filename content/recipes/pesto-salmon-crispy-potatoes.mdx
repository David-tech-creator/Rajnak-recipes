A beautiful dish with stuffed salmon fillet and crispy roasted potato slices with a lemon creme fraiche sauce.

## Instructions

Put the salmon in a bowl lubricated with olive oil, cut a decent deep slice into the fillet, fill the slice with Philadelphia cheese, then put a layer of pesto over it and over the pesto to put sun-dried tomatoes. For this we made split potatoes, cannonballs and then we made a lemon sauce, you buy a jar of Creme Fraiche and tear the shell on a lemon in and sprinkle in a little lemon juice as well, this you have for the split ice cream and salmon.
