---
title: Spicy Tuna & Avocado Toast
date: '2022-04-05'
category: Quick & Easy
image: /images/recipes/spicy-tuna-avocado-toast.jpg
prepTime: '10 mins'
cookTime: '5 mins'
servings: 2
kitchenNote: Toast hot, avocado just-smashed. Don't assemble early.
signoff: Tuck in.
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 4 slices sourdough or country bread
  - 1 can good tuna in oil (about 160 g), drained
  - 1 ripe avocado
  - 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  - 1 tbsp sriracha, more to taste
  - 1 tsp lemon juice
  - 1 tsp soy sauce
  - 1 spring onion, thinly sliced
  - 1 small handful cherry tomatoes, halved
  - Black sesame seeds, to finish
  - Salt and black pepper
instructions:
  - Toast the bread until golden and crisp on both sides.
  - In a small bowl, flake the drained tuna and mix with the mayonnaise, sriracha, lemon juice, and soy sauce. Taste for heat and salt.
  - Smash the avocado with a fork, season with a pinch of salt and a squeeze of lemon, and spread it thickly over the warm toast.
  - Pile the spicy tuna on top, scatter with cherry tomatoes and spring onion, finish with sesame seeds and a few extra drops of sriracha.
---

A five-minute lunch built around the spicy-tuna flavours of a sushi bar. Use the best tinned tuna you can find — the rest is just assembly.
