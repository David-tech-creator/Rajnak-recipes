title: "Breakfast: Chia Porridge with Oatmeal and Raspberries Wonderful Start to the Morning That Can Be Prepared the Day Before."
date: '2026-05-23'
category: Quick & Easy
image: /images/recipes/chia-porridge-oatmeal-raspberries.jpg
ingredients:
  - 4 tablespoons chia seeds
  - 2 dl unsweetened almond milk
  - 1 teaspoon vanilla powder
  - 115 g frozen raspberries
  - 1 tablespoon agave syrup
  - 1 dl natural skyr or yoghurt
  - "Oatmeal:"
  - 125 g oatmeal
  - 1 tablespoon butter for frying
  - 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  - 1 pinch of salt
  - "Accessories:"
  - 4 tablespoons natural skyr or yogurt
  - 10 raspberries
  - 6 blackberries
  - 2 tablespoons puffed quinoa
  - "Do this:"
prepTime: ''
cookTime: ''
servings: 4
featured: false
---

1. Stir together chia seeds, milk and vanilla powder in a bowl. Cover and

leave to cool until the next morning.

2. Mix the cold chia mixture with frozen raspberries and syrup in a blender,

so that it becomes a creamy porridge. Stir in skyr / yogurt.

3. Oatmeal: Roast the groats in butter in a pan on low heat for about five

minutes. Add the syrup, salt and stir. Roast for a few more minutes.

4. Let cool on a baking sheet.

5. Serving: Distribute the oatmeal in the bottom of a glass / jar. Top with

porridge and top with skyr / yoghurt, berries and puffed quinoa.
