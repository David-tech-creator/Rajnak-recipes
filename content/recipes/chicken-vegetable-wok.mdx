A quick and easy chicken wok with frozen vegetables and rice — perfect for busy weeknights.

## Instructions

1. Fry chicken cubes in olive oil until brown.
2. Cook frozen vegetables in microwave for 8-10 minutes. Pour off water.
3. Cook rice for 10-15 minutes.
4. Salt and pepper the chicken.
5. Pour on vegetables and rice. Mix.
6. If needed, add half a deciliter of liquid.
7. Bake 5 minutes.
8. Add soy sauce to your plate when serving.
