Crispy breaded chicken fillets served with a creamy cheese sauce made from Philadelphia, grated cheese, and creme fraiche with a hint of mustard.

## Instructions

1. Clear the chicken fillet of white fat. Divide into small oval pieces. Salt them.
2. Bread with flour, then dip in egg mixed with a little milk. Roll in breadcrumbs.
3. Fry in oil on both sides until nice color and cooked through on medium heat.

## Cheese Sauce

1. Mix Philadelphia, grated cheese, creme fraiche, salt, pepper, and 1-2 tsp mustard together.
2. Serve alongside the paned chicken.
