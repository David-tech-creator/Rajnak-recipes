A classic Hungarian beef stew rich with paprika and slow-cooked until the meat is tender. Serve with homemade nokedli (spatzli) for the authentic experience.

## Instructions

1. Cut the meat into 1-2cm cubes.
2. Fry the onion in oil until it gets color.
3. Sprinkle with paprika powder (be careful not to burn it) and add the meat immediately.
4. Stir for a few minutes so the meat is cooked on all sides. Salt it.
5. Pour enough water to cover the meat. Steam well covered.
6. Cooking time is approximately 2 hours without a pressure cooker, or 40 minutes with a pressure cooker.
7. You can add 1-2 tomatoes and/or green peppers. Cook until soft.
8. The stew should not be too thin or too thick.
9. Serve with spatzli ("nokedli"). Decorate with parsley.
