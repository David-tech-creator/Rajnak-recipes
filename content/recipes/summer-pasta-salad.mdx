---
title: Summer Pasta Salad
date: '2026-05-23'
category: Quick & Easy
image: /images/recipes/summer-pasta-salad.jpg
prepTime: '15 mins'
cookTime: '10 mins'
servings: 4
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 400 g short pasta (fusilli, farfalle, or penne)
  - 250 g cherry tomatoes, halved
  - 1 small cucumber, diced
  - 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  - 150 g feta cheese, crumbled
  - 100 g black or kalamata olives, pitted
  - 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  - 1 handful fresh basil leaves, torn
  - 4 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  - 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  - 1 clove garlic, grated
  - Salt and black pepper, to taste
instructions:
  - Cook the pasta in well-salted boiling water until just al dente. Drain, rinse briefly under cold water to stop the cooking, and toss with a little olive oil so it doesn't stick.
  - In a large bowl whisk together the olive oil, vinegar, mustard, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  - Add the cooled pasta to the dressing along with the tomatoes, cucumber, pepper, olives, and red onion. Toss thoroughly so everything is coated.
  - Just before serving, fold in the crumbled feta and torn basil. Taste once more for salt and a splash more vinegar.
---

The easiest summer lunch and the most reliable potluck contribution. Make it an hour ahead so the flavours marry, but add the feta and basil at the last minute.
