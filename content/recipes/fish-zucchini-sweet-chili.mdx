Steamed red mullet on a bed of cherry tomatoes, courgette, and artichoke hearts with thyme and chilli.

## Instructions

1. Halve tomatoes, cube courgette, quarter artichokes.
2. Stir olive oil into vegetables with thyme leaves, season with salt and pepper, and spread in perforated cooking container.
3. Rinse fish, pat dry, cut into 8 equal pieces, and sprinkle with salt.
4. Place fish on vegetables with chilli pepper.
5. Steam at 100°C for 10 minutes.
