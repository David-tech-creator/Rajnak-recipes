ingredients:
  - 600 g red mullet fillet (or other firm white fish)
  - 250 g cherry tomatoes, halved
  - 1 medium courgette, cut into 1-cm cubes
  - 180 g jarred artichoke hearts, drained and quartered
  - 2–3 tbsp olive oil
  - 6 sprigs thyme
  - 1 tsp shredded chilli pepper (or 0.5 tsp chilli flakes)
  - 2 dl basmati rice
  - 4 dl water
  - 3 tbsp sweet chili sauce
  - Salt and black pepper, to taste
instructions:
  - Rinse the rice, then cook with the water and a pinch of salt — bring to a boil, cover, reduce to low, and simmer 12 minutes. Rest off the heat for 5.
  - Heat the oven to 200°C. Toss the tomatoes, courgette and artichoke hearts with the olive oil, the leaves from three thyme sprigs, salt and pepper.
  - Spread the vegetables in a baking dish and roast for 10 minutes.
  - Rinse the fish, pat dry, and cut into 8 equal pieces. Salt lightly.
  - Lay the fish on top of the vegetables, scatter the chilli pepper and remaining thyme over the top, and return to the oven for 8–10 minutes, until the fish is just opaque.
  - Plate the rice, spoon the vegetables and fish on top, and drizzle with sweet chili sauce.
---

A bright, weeknight one-pan that comes together in 20 minutes. The sweet chili sauce is the trick — it lifts the whole plate.
