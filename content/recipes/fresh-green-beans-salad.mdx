---
title: Fresh Green Beans Salad
date: '2026-05-23'
category: Quick & Easy
image: /images/recipes/fresh-green-beans-salad.jpg
prepTime: '15 mins'
cookTime: '10 mins'
servings: 4
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 400 g haricots verts (fine green beans), trimmed
  - 150 g smoked bacon, cut into small cubes
  - 2 oranges, supremed
  - 50 g pine nuts
  - 4 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  - 1.5 tsp Dijon mustard
  - 1 tsp dried herb-salad mix (or herbes de Provence)
  - Salt and black pepper, to taste
instructions:
  - Bring a pot of well-salted water to a boil. Blanch the haricots verts for 4 minutes, until just tender but still bright. Drain and plunge into ice water to stop the cooking. Drain again.
  - Fry the bacon cubes in a dry pan over medium heat until crisp and golden. Lift onto kitchen paper.
  - In the same pan with the bacon fat removed, toast the pine nuts briefly until just golden — watch closely.
  - Whisk the olive oil, vinegar, mustard, herb-salad mix, salt and pepper to a smooth dressing.
  - In a large bowl, toss the beans with the bacon, orange segments, and most of the pine nuts. Drizzle over the dressing and toss again.
  - Scatter the remaining pine nuts on top and serve at room temperature.
---

A light, brilliant salad that holds together beautifully — bright green beans, smoky bacon, sweet orange, and toasted pine nuts. Excellent next to grilled meat or as a starter.
