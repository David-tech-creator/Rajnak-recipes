A light and elegant appetizer of smooth avocado mousse served in the avocado shells and topped with fresh shrimps.

## Instructions

1. Cut the avocado in half and remove the seed.
2. Scrape out the flesh with a spoon, keeping the shells for serving.
3. Add the avocado pieces in a mixer with 2 dl sour cream.
4. Add salt and pepper to taste.
5. Mix until smooth.
6. Spoon the mousse back into the avocado shells.
7. Decorate with fresh shrimps.
