ingredients:
  - 1 large egg
  - 3 tsp caster sugar
  - A few drops of fresh lemon juice
  - A pinch of salt (for the white)
instructions:
  - Crack the egg and carefully separate the yolk from the white into two bowls.
  - Whisk the egg white with a pinch of salt until it holds stiff peaks.
  - In the other bowl, beat the yolk with the sugar and lemon juice until pale, foamy, and slightly thickened.
  - Spoon the yellow lemon cream into a small glass and top with the snowy whipped white.
  - Eat immediately with a teaspoon, ideally with the Sunday newspaper.
---

A Swedish Sunday morning treat from Pappa's childhood — whipped egg whites alongside a sweet lemony egg yolk cream. Tiny, simple, and finished in under ten minutes.
