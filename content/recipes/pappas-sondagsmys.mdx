A simple Swedish Sunday treat -- whipped egg whites and a sweet lemony egg yolk cream.

## Instructions

1. Take 1-2 eggs.
2. Separate the yellow from the white.
3. Whisk the egg white until hard.
4. Add 3 teaspoons sprinkle sugar and a few drops of lemon into the egg yolk.
5. Whip until slightly foamy on top.
6. Serve!
