title: "Mamma's Mashed Potatoes"
date: '2022-03-15'

prepTime: '10 mins'
cookTime: '25 mins'
servings: 4
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 1 kg floury potatoes (King Edward or Bintje), peeled
  - 1 tsp salt, for the cooking water
  - 80 g butter, in cubes
  - 1.5 dl whole milk, warm
  - 0.5 tsp salt, to taste
  - White pepper, to taste
  - Pinch of freshly grated nutmeg (optional)
instructions:
  - Peel the potatoes and cut into evenly sized pieces, about 4 cm. Rinse briefly under cold water.
  - Put them in a saucepan, cover with cold water, add the salt, and bring to the boil.
  - Lower to a brisk simmer and cook for 18–20 minutes, until a knife slides in without resistance.
  - Drain well and return the potatoes to the dry pan over low heat for a minute to steam off any water.
  - Mash with a potato ricer or hand masher (never a food processor — it goes gluey).
  - Beat in the butter cube by cube, then the warm milk in a steady stream, until smooth. Season with salt, pepper, and a whisper of nutmeg.
---

The mash that goes with meatballs, with gulasch, with everything. Floury potatoes, real butter, warm milk — no shortcuts. A ricer makes them lighter than air.
