A Japanese feast featuring sushi, katsu, and tempura all in one spread.

## Instructions

### Sushi
1. Rinse rice. Cook (1.5 cup water per cup rice, 4dl rice 4 cups).
2. Add sushi seasoning to rice. Cool rice.
3. Cut avocado and cucumber.

### Katsu
1. Dip meat in thick sesame seeds.
2. Mix 2 eggs + 1.25dl water + 1dl flour with chopsticks.
3. Salt and pepper meat.
4. Pour panko into deep dish. Dip meat in katsu batter then panko.
5. Fry in vegetable oil until golden brown.
6. Crush sesame seeds with mortar.

### Tempura
1. Cut zucchini (less than 1cm thick), peel carrots, scale mushrooms.
2. Mix 100g tempura mix with 160cc water, leave a little clumsy.
3. Tempura sauce: 1 part sauce, 2 part water (kikkoman, soup base for noodles).
