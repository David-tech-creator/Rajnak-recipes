A lighter variation of the traditional Hungarian porkolt, made with chicken drumsticks or mushrooms instead of beef. Same wonderful paprika flavor, shorter cooking time.

## Instructions

1. Cut the chicken into pieces (or clean and slice the mushrooms).
2. Fry the onion in oil until it gets color.
3. Sprinkle with paprika powder (be careful not to burn it) and add the chicken or mushroom immediately.
4. Stir for a few minutes so the meat is cooked on all sides. Salt it.
5. Pour enough water to cover the meat. Steam well covered.
6. Cook until the chicken is tender and cooked through, or until mushrooms are soft.
7. You can add 1-2 tomatoes and/or green peppers.
8. The stew should not be too thin or too thick.
9. Serve with spatzli ("nokedli"). Decorate with parsley.
