Swedish "Poor Knights" - a simple and delicious way to use up stale bread, similar to French toast.

## Instructions

Take old, dry bread. Add warm milk until it sucks up the milk. Then put in beaten egg. Then fry in hot oil (a lot of oil) on high heat until golden.
