---
title: Swedish Shrimp Sandwich
date: '2023-12-01'
category: Sandwiches
image: /images/shrimp-sandwich.jpg
ingredients:
  - 4 slices of bread
  - 200g peeled shrimp
  - 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  - 1 tbsp dill, chopped
  - 1 hard-boiled egg, sliced
  - Lettuce leaves
  - Lemon wedges for serving
instructions:
  - Mix shrimp with mayonnaise and dill.
  - Toast the bread lightly if desired.
  - Place lettuce on bread slices, top with shrimp mixture.
  - Garnish with egg slices and serve with lemon wedges.
prepTime: 15 mins
cookTime: 0 mins
servings: 2
---

A classic Swedish open-faced sandwich that's perfect for lunch or a light dinner. The combination of fresh shrimp, creamy mayonnaise, and fragrant dill creates a delightful flavor profile.

## Variations

- Add a slice of avocado for extra creaminess
- Sprinkle with fish roe for a luxurious touch
- Use rye bread for a more traditional approach
