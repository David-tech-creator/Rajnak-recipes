title: Swedish Shrimp Sandwich (Räksmörgås)

prepTime: '20 mins'
cookTime: '0 mins'
servings: 2
kitchenNote: Build it tall. Dill on top, always.
signoff: Smaklig måltid.
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 2 large slices sourdough or dark rye bread
  - 2 tbsp salted butter, softened
  - 4 large leaves of butter lettuce
  - 2 hard-boiled eggs, peeled and sliced
  - 300 g cold-water shrimp, peeled (skalade räkor)
  - 4 tbsp good mayonnaise
  - 1 tsp lemon juice
  - 1 tbsp fresh dill, chopped, plus extra to garnish
  - 4 thin slices cucumber
  - 1 lemon, cut into wedges
  - 2 tsp löjrom or red caviar, optional
  - Salt and white pepper
instructions:
  - Toast the bread lightly if you like, then butter generously to the edges so the lettuce has something to stick to.
  - Lay two leaves of lettuce on each slice and arrange sliced egg over the top, slightly overlapping.
  - Stir the shrimp gently with the mayonnaise, lemon juice, chopped dill, a pinch of salt, and a few grinds of white pepper. Don't crush them.
  - Pile the shrimp mixture in a generous mound on top of the egg layer — it should look almost too tall.
  - Garnish each sandwich with cucumber slices, a small spoon of löjrom if using, a sprig of dill, and a lemon wedge on the side.
---

The classic Swedish open-faced sandwich and the test of every café in the country. The mountain of shrimp is the point — go heavy.
