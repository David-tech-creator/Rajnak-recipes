An elegant presentation plate of smoked salmon, boiled eggs, and caviar. A staple of the Rajnak family Christmas and Easter table.

## Instructions

1. Boil the eggs and let them cool. Slice in halves.
2. Arrange lettuce leaves on a serving plate.
3. Place smoked salmon slices and egg halves on the lettuce.
4. Top with caviar.
5. Garnish with lemon wedges and serve.
