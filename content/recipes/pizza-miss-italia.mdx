servings: 2

ingredients:
  - 1 ready-made pizza base (or 250 g pizza dough, stretched)
  - 4 tbsp tomato passata
  - 150 g fresh mozzarella, torn
  - 80 g Parma ham, thinly sliced
  - 1 large handful rocket
  - 12 cherry tomatoes, halved
  - 30 g parmesan, shaved
  - 1 tbsp truffle oil
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - Sea salt and black pepper, to taste
instructions:
  - Heat the oven to its highest setting (250 °C, with a pizza stone or heavy tray inside if you have one).
  - Stretch the pizza base on a lightly floured peel or sheet of parchment. Spread the passata thinly, leaving a 1-cm border.
  - Scatter over the mozzarella, season lightly, and slide the pizza into the oven. Bake for 8–10 minutes, until the crust is blistered and the cheese melted.
  - Pull the pizza out. Drape the Parma ham over the hot surface so it warms and softens but doesn't cook.
  - Pile the rocket on top, scatter the cherry tomatoes and parmesan shavings, and drizzle with truffle oil and olive oil.
  - Finish with a crack of black pepper and serve straight away, cut into wedges.
---

The classic Italian pizza with everything good on top: salty Parma ham, peppery rocket, sweet tomatoes, and a hit of truffle oil. The trick is adding the toppings off the heat so the ham stays silky.
