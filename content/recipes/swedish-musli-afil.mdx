date: '2026-05-23'

image: /images/recipes/swedish-musli-afil.jpg
prepTime: '5 mins'
cookTime: '0 mins'
servings: 2
signoff: Smaklig måltid.
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 4 dl A-fil, filmjölk, or kefir
  - 2 dl rolled oats
  - 0.5 dl cornflakes
  - 2 tbsp raisins
  - 2 tbsp toasted coconut flakes
  - 1 ripe banana, sliced
  - 1 slice fresh pineapple, diced
  - 0.5 small papaya, diced
  - 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup, optional
  - A pinch of ground cinnamon
instructions:
  - Pour the A-fil or kefir into two deep bowls.
  - Layer the oats and cornflakes over the cultured milk.
  - Top with banana, pineapple, and papaya.
  - Sprinkle with raisins and toasted coconut, drizzle with honey if using, and finish with a pinch of cinnamon.
---

The Swedish breakfast that doubles as a snack on hot days. A-fil is a sour cultured milk closer to drinkable yogurt — any thick kefir works in its place.
