---
title: Juicy Upside-Down Plum Cake
date: '2026-05-23'
category: Found Recipes
image: /images/recipes/upside-down-plum-cake.jpg
ingredients:
  - 150g Butter
  - 1 dl Raw cane sugar
  - 5 Plums
  - 2 dl Powdered sugar
  - 2 Eggs
  - 3 dl Flour
  - 1½ tsp Baking powder
  - 1½ tsp Vanilla powder
  - 2 dl Milk
  - 2 dl Crème fraiche
prepTime: ''
cookTime: ''
servings: 4
featured: false
---

1. Set the oven to 175 °.

2. Melt 50 g butter and mix with raw sugar.

3. Cover the bottom of a mold, about 24 cm in diameter, with the mixture.

4. Take out seed of the plums, slice them and spread them on the bottom of

the pan. The pattern you make here will then be the top of the cake.

5. Whisk 100 g butter and porous sugar.

6. Add the eggs one at a time.

7. Mix wheat flour, baking powder and vanilla powder and stir into the

batter together with the milk.

8. Pour the batter over the plums and smooth to the surface.

9. Bake in the lower part of the oven for about 45 minutes.

10. Let the cake cool in the mold for about 20 min.

11.Turn the cake upside down on a plate. Serve with a dash of crème fraiche.
