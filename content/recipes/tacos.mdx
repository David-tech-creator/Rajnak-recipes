title: Tacos

image: /images/recipes/tacos.jpg
prepTime: '20 mins'
cookTime: '15 mins'
servings: 4
kitchenNote: Warm the shells. Cold shells crack.
signoff: Buen provecho.
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 600 g minced beef (or pork)
  - 1 large yellow onion, finely chopped
  - 2 cloves garlic, minced
  - 1 tbsp neutral oil
  - 2 tbsp taco seasoning (or 1 tsp paprika, 1 tsp cumin, 0.5 tsp oregano, 0.5 tsp chili)
  - 1 dl water
  - 1 tbsp tomato paste
  - 12 taco shells or 12 small tortillas, warmed
  - 2 ripe tomatoes, diced
  - 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  - 1 small cucumber, diced
  - 1 ripe avocado, sliced or mashed
  - 2 dl sweetcorn
  - 150 g cheddar or grated cheese
  - 2 dl crème fraîche or sour cream
  - 1 small jar of salsa
  - 1 small bunch fresh coriander
  - 1 lime, cut into wedges
instructions:
  - Heat the oil in a wide pan and fry the chopped onion over medium heat until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes. Add the garlic and cook another minute.
  - Add the minced meat, breaking it up with a wooden spoon, and brown it well — let it actually catch on the pan for colour.
  - Stir in the taco seasoning, tomato paste, and water, season with salt, and simmer for 10 minutes until thick.
  - Set out all the toppings — tomato, red onion, cucumber, avocado, corn, cheese, sour cream, salsa, coriander, lime — in small bowls on the table.
  - Warm the taco shells or tortillas. Let everyone build their own.
---

The classic Swedish-style Friday taco night (fredagstaco) — a wall of bowls and shells, everyone assembling their own. The Swedes eat more tacos per capita than anyone outside Mexico.
