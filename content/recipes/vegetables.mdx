---
title: Oven-Roasted Seasonal Vegetables
date: '2023-10-20'
category: Family Recipes
image: /images/recipes/vegetables.svg
prepTime: '10 mins'
cookTime: '30 mins'
servings: 4
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 2 medium carrots, peeled and cut into batons
  - 1 small head broccoli, in florets
  - 1 red bell pepper, in chunks
  - 1 medium zucchini, in thick half-moons
  - 1 red onion, in wedges
  - 12 cherry tomatoes
  - 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  - 2 cloves garlic, smashed
  - 1 tsp dried thyme or herbes de Provence
  - Flaky salt
  - Black pepper, to taste
  - 1 tbsp lemon juice or balsamic vinegar, to finish
  - A few fresh herbs (parsley, basil), optional
instructions:
  - Heat the oven to 220 °C. Line a large baking tray with parchment.
  - Cut the vegetables into similar-sized pieces so they cook evenly. Spread them in a single layer on the tray.
  - Drizzle generously with olive oil, scatter the garlic and dried herbs, and season with salt and pepper. Toss with your hands to coat every piece.
  - Roast for 25 to 30 minutes, tossing once halfway through, until everything is tender with golden-brown edges.
  - Finish with a squeeze of lemon or a drizzle of balsamic, scatter with fresh herbs if using, and serve hot or warm.
---

The all-purpose vegetable side that works alongside almost any main. The high heat caramelises the edges — don't crowd the tray, or they'll steam instead of roast.
