ingredients:
  - Mixed seasonal vegetables
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - Salt and pepper to taste
  - Fresh herbs (optional)
instructions:
  - Wash and chop vegetables into even pieces.
  - Toss with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  - Roast in oven at 200°C for 15 minutes or until tender.
  - Sprinkle with fresh herbs before serving if desired.
prepTime: 10 mins
cookTime: 15 mins
servings: 4
---

A simple way to prepare delicious roasted vegetables that can accompany any main dish. The high heat caramelizes the natural sugars in the vegetables, enhancing their flavor.

## Vegetable Suggestions

- Root vegetables: carrots, parsnips, beets
- Cruciferous: broccoli, cauliflower, Brussels sprouts
- Others: bell peppers, zucchini, eggplant
