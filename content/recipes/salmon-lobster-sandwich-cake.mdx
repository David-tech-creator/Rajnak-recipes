This must be the queen of sandwich cakes! Creamy, majestic and beautiful. Feel free to let it rest in the fridge.

Ingredients (8 servings):

• 9 disc (s) landing bread

Decorative cream

• 2 dl sour cream

• 0.5 dl mayonnaise

Filling

• 3 st egg , hard boiled

• 350 grams crayfish tails , peeled (let drain if they are in layers)

• 1 dl mayonnaise

• 1 dl sour cream

• 2 tsp horseradish

• 1 st lemon (s)

• 1 dl dill , chopped

• salt

• black pepper

Garnish

• 200 grams cold-smoked salmon

• lemon (s)

• dill

• lettuce leaf

1. Lay out 3 slices of bread on a tray or platter. Cut out a roundel with a plate as a template. Do the same with the other

loaves, so that you have three round bases.

2. Chop eggs and crayfish tails. Mix mayonnaise, crème fraiche, horseradish, lemon juice and peel and dill. Mix in eggs

and crayfish and season with salt and pepper.

Spread half of the crayfish mixture on the first round. Lay the next round over and spread out the rest of the crayfish

mixture. Put on the last round.

4. Mix with the garnish and spread over the cake. Wrap and refrigerate for a few hours, preferably overnight.

5. Before serving, garnish the cake with salmon, lemon, dill and lettuce leaves.
