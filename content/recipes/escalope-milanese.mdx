Classic breaded veal escalope, known in Hungarian as "rantott husi," fried until golden and crispy. Served with oven-roasted root vegetables.

## Instructions

1. Pound the veal slices thin.
2. Prepare 3 bowls: one with flour, one with whisked eggs, and one with breadcrumbs.
3. Dip the veal first in flour, then in eggs, then in breadcrumbs.
4. Fry in lots of rapeseed oil until crispy and a nice color on medium heat.
5. Roast potatoes, parsnip, and carrots in the oven until tender and golden.
6. Serve the escalope alongside the roasted vegetables.
