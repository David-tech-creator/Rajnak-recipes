---
title: Garlic-Scented Chicken
date: '2022-04-21'
category: Found Recipes
image: /images/recipes/garlic-scented-chicken.jpg
prepTime: '10 mins'
cookTime: '30 mins'
servings: 4
featured: false
ingredients:
  - 700 g chicken fillets
  - 3 cloves garlic, pressed
  - 1 tsp salt
  - 0.5 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  - 2 dl grated cheese (Västerbotten or aged cheddar)
  - 4 dl crème fraîche
  - 1 chicken stock cube
  - Boiled potatoes or rice, to serve
  - Steamed broccoli or green beans, to serve
instructions:
  - Heat the oven to 200°C. Pat the chicken fillets dry and place them in a single layer in an ovenproof dish.
  - Press the garlic evenly over the top, then season with salt and pepper.
  - In a bowl, stir the grated cheese into the crème fraîche. Crumble in the stock cube and mix well.
  - Pour the cheese mixture over the chicken so each fillet is well covered.
  - Bake on the middle rack for about 30 minutes, until the chicken is cooked through and the top is bubbly and golden.
  - Rest 5 minutes, then serve with boiled potatoes or rice and a green vegetable.
---

The Swedish weeknight comfort dish — chicken under a blanket of cheese and garlicky crème fraîche, ready in 40 minutes. Freezes well too.
