Oven-baked chicken fillets in a creamy garlic, cheese, and cream fraiche sauce. Works well to freeze.

## Instructions

1. Thaw chicken fillets.
2. Heat oven to 200 degrees.
3. Place the fillets on an ovenproof dish.
4. Press over the garlic. Salt and pepper.
5. Mix cheese and creme fraiche. Crumble in the broth dice.
6. Pour all over the chicken fillets.
7. Bake for about 30 minutes.
